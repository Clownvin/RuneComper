You're Not My Real Mah is an achievement that requires the player to unlock all chronicle entries associated with elder chronicles, which requires catching 100 elder chronicles (10 chronicles for each memory). They must be handed in to the monument in Guthix's shrine or offered in the inventory to count towards the achievement. If the player hands them into the energy rift, they will not get achievement progress.

Enhanced elder chronicles collected from the chronicle attraction prayer do still count towards the achievement.

Elder chronicles can be captured by training Divination at Elder Halls within Freneskae. The pontifex observation ring (or its upgrade) is notably helpful, as it provides an increased spawn rate of chronicle fragments. The elder divination outfit can be extremely beneficial as well, as it provides a 7% chance to double the chronicles gathered. The inferior energy, chronicle, or memory outfit can also suffice, albeit with only a 5% chance to grant double chronicles. If a player uses a divine-o-matic vacuum configured to consume both energy and memory, it will make this process considerably more afk.

Players with access to Chronicle Attraction Curse and a Grace of the elves with the Dark Facet of Grace enchantment (or an ancient elven ritual shard, though this requires remembering to use the shard every few minutes) will be able to maintain unlimited prayer points. Without a grace of the elves, super restore potions can be used to keep prayer topped off.

If a player is also yet to complete the Mahjarrat Memories achievement and plans on doing so in the future, it may be beneficial to gather the required memories in the Elder Halls so they can also begin obtaining Elder chronicles so progress can be made toward both requirements at the same time.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name of this achievement is a reference to the phrase "You're not my real ma", a defiant phrase often spoken in association with adoption.
