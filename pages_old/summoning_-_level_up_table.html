LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Summoning contributes to a player's combat level
  • Each level in Summoning adds one point to a player's pool of Summoning points
  • Each level in Summoning slightly increases the experience earned from Clan Citadel obelisks
1
  • Summon spirit wolves and use Spirit Wolf scroll (Howl)s
  • Own the following pets: bulldogs, dalmatians, greyhounds, labradors, sheepdogs, terriers, baby troll, eek
  • Ability to raise your Summoning level to 5
  • Summon cub bloodragers and use tier 1 Sundering Strike scrolls in Daemonheim
  • Summon class 1 clay familiars in Stealing Creation
  • Ability to raise your Summoning level to 99
  • Ability to complete the Dagon Bye mystery, sign a Pact with Dagon, and create binding contracts
2
  • Summon cub deathslingers and use tier 1 Poisonous Shot scrolls in Daemonheim
3
  • Summon cub stormbringers and use tier 1 Snaring Wave scrolls in Daemonheim

  • Summon dreadfowls and use Dreadfowl scroll (Dreadfowl Strike)s
  • Summon meerkats and use Meerkat scroll (Fetch Casket)s
  • Own the following pets: creeping hands (with 5 Slayer), minitrices (with 25 Slayer), baby basilisks (with 40 Slayer), baby kurasks (with 70 Slayer), abyssal minions (with 85 Slayer), and rune guardians (after Rune Mechanics)
  • Required to complete the Fremennik Province easy tasks
5

  • Summon cub hoardstalkers and use tier 1 Aptitude scrolls in Daemonheim
