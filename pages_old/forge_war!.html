Forge War! can be obtained from excavating at various excavation hotspots at the Warforge Dig Site. Recovering this page and talking to Zanik afterwards at the dig site entrance will complete the Forge War! mystery.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Forge War!.

Bandos gone. Was fighting green god but lost. Dorgeshuun betray and he bury them. We not betray but he still cave in Warforge. Not know why. We stuck here.

Orcs gone. Ogres gone. Hobgobs gone. Ourgs gone. Just us gobbos left. Tribes separate. We name selves! I am Dumbface!

We build barriers, keep others out. No more games, but still lot of teams. We build more barriers.

Food getting scarce down here. Water might be contaminate. We send out raiding party. Most not come back. Least we need less food now.

Need stuff to trade with Mr Dwarf. He one with weapons. He bless our victory. He prized commodity.

This is the Forge War. In the Warforge. Yurkolgokh will be winners. Yurkolgokh will be supreme. This is final game! We appease Bandos, he return and set us free. It only way out.

Dumbface of the Yurkolgokh
