Civil War I is the second miniquest in the Legacy of Zamorak quest series concerning the Zamorakian Civil War. It follows the events of the Daughter of Chaos quest. It serves as a narrative framework for the Gross Misconduct achievement and took over its role as a requirement for the tier 3 upgrade of the Infernal Puzzle Box.

Letter from Anne Dimitri[edit | edit source]

Adrasteia will tell you that Anne Dimitri has resurfaced and sent her a letter requesting you to meet her at the usual meeting spot near Quercus north of the Grand Exchange shortcut from Edgeville.

Meeting Anne Dimitri[edit | edit source]

Anne will tell you she is no longer playing the Saradominist spy but she is now the sardonic Zamorakian cultist. She will go on to tell you she has new information about the civil war because she has been placing monitoring devices throughout Forinthry, but there's a catch. She will only tell you the information if you help her weaken K'ril's forces by defeating some demons in the Wilderness. This will require you to complete the Gross Misconduct achievement by killing 144 demons in the Wilderness. Once you have completed this achievement talk to Anne Dimitri again and she will hand over the recording.

Jerrod's Offer[edit | edit source]

When you view the recording you will see Hazeel, Enakhra, Zemouregal, and General Khazard at their forward Mahjarrat camp waiting for Bilrach to show a meeting that he called for. They are discussing their thoughts on Moia when Bilrach appears with Jerrod and two werewolves. Jerrod says he is willing to join the Mahjarrat forces in the civil war in return for their help overthrowing the vampyres once the war is over. The Mahjarrat begin to discuss the proposition when Bilrach cuts them off to say he has already made a decision. They will accept Jerrod's offer and align with the werewolves.

The recording ends and Anne Dimitri clarifies that the Mahjarrat forces are aligning with a group of werewolves but insists there is no reason to pass this information to Adrasteia.

Miniquest complete!
