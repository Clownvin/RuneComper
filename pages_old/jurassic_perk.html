Jurassic Perk is an achievement that requires the player to discover all the totem pieces on Anachronia. The Remototem pieces are not required to complete this achievement.

Totem pieces required to complete this achievement include:

Totem Effect How to obtain
Treasure Reduces the number of steps required to complete a clue scroll by one. Top: Obtained randomly while doing Big Game Hunter.
Middle: Obtained randomly while on Anachronia Agility Course.
Base: Obtained by pulling up 20 ancient zygomites.
Navigation Reduces the duration of voyages from player-owned port by 15%. Top: Found in rubble east of Laniakea (on the south-eastern peninsula).
Middle: Obtained by pulling up 40 ancient zygomites.
Base: Found by searching an overgrown idol on Anachronia after cutting it.
Crystal Reduces elven clan lock-out period while pickpocketing by 25%. Top: Obtained randomly on Anachronia Agility Course.
Middle: Found in rubble in ruins north-east of base camp, south of Feral Dinosaurs.
Base: Bought from Herby Werby reward shop for 200 spirit points.
Abyss Allows teleportation to itself when placed. Top: Bought from Herby Werby reward shop for 200 spirit points.
Middle: Obtained randomly while doing Big Game Hunter.
Base: Found by searching rubble near the northern building at the southern ruins on Anachronia north of the yellow salamanders.
Auras Reduces the cooldown of auras by 15%. Top: Found by pulling up all 60 ancient zygomites.
Middle: Obtained randomly while killing slayer monsters on the island (can be off-task).
Base: Obtained randomly while on Anachronia Agility Course.
Intimidation Allows skipping the kill count requirement to access God Wars Dungeon generals (excluding Nex). Top: Found by searching rubble slightly north of the ruins on the north-eastern part of Anachronia.
Middle: Rewarded by Laniakea upon completing the Helping Laniakea miniquest.
Base: Obtained randomly while killing slayer monsters on the island (can be off-task).
Summoning Increases the duration of summoned familiars by 16 minutes. Top: Obtained randomly while on Anachronia Agility Course.
Middle: Obtained randomly while killing slayer monsters on the island (can be off-task).
Base: Obtained randomly while doing Big Game Hunter.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement's name is a reference to the media franchise Jurassic Park.
