The clean necklace is obtained by cleaning uncleaned finds in the Varrock Museum which requires completion of the The Dig Site quest. Finding it is very rare and can easily take over an hour. Players must then talk to one of the archeologists in the cleaning finds area of the museum. They will then teach the player how to enchant the necklace to make a Dig Site pendant. This also gives the I Dig This Transportation achievement.
