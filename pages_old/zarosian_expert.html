Zarosian Expert is an achievement that requires the player to have restored all 52 Zarosian artefacts from the Kharid-et and Senntisten Dig Sites. There are nine artefacts in Zarosian I, ten artefacts in Zarosian II, nine artefacts in Zarosian III, six artefacts in Zarosian IV, six artefacts in Zarosian V, six artefacts in Zarosian VI, and six artefacts in Zarosian VII.

Completing this achievement rewards the player with the title, Zarosian Expert [Name].
