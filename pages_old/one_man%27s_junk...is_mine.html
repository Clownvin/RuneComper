One Man's Junk...Is Mine is an achievement that requires the player to fully loot Movario's base. The room containing all the loot can be accessed by entering a tunnel in Movario's study. Movario's base contains 100 each of coal, death runes, fire runes, and magic logs.

To complete the achievement in a single trip, players can disassemble the coal and the magic logs, or use a beast of burden. Players may also use a steel ore box or better for the coal, and a magic wood box or better for the magic logs.

Attempting to use magic notepaper on the coal or logs presents the following message, "You cannot use magic notepaper in this situation."
