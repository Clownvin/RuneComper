LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Increases the yield from herb patches
  • Increases the chance of getting the Brains pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
1
  • Plant Potato seeds and grow Potatoes in an allotment patch
  • Ability to raise your Farming level to 5
  • Nurture basic Evil tree saplings
  • Plant Cave potato seeds and grow Cave potatoes in Daemonheim
  • Pick Salve nettles in Daemonheim
  • Access Vinesweeper
  • Ability to raise your Farming level to 99
2
  • Plant Marigold seeds and grow Marigolds in a flower patch
3
  • Plant Barley seeds and grow Barley in a hops patch
4
  • Plant Hammerstone seeds and grow Hammerstone hops in a hops patch
5
  • Plant Onion seeds and grow Onions in an allotment patch
  • One of the requirements for Perils of Ice Mountain
6
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
7
  • Plant Cabbage seeds and grow Cabbages in an allotment patch
  • Nurture Evil oak tree saplings
  • Plant Sagewort seeds and grow Sageworts in Daemonheim
8
  • Plant Asgarnian seeds and grow Asgarnian hops in a hops patch
9
  • Plant Guam seeds and grow Guam in a herb patch
10
  • Plant Redberry seeds and grow Redberries in a bush patch
  • Pick Wildercress in Daemonheim
11
  • Plant Rosemary seeds and grow Rosemary in a flower patch
12
  • Plant Tomato seeds and grow Tomatoes in an allotment patch
13
  • Plant Jute seeds and grow Jute fibres in a hops patch
  • One of the requirements for the Seers' Village easy tasks
14
  • Plant Marrentill seeds and grow Marrentill in a herb patch
15
  • Plant Acorns and grow Oak trees in a tree patch
  • Nurture Evil willow tree saplings
  • One of the requirements for the Varrock elite tasks
16
  • Plant Yanillian seeds and grow Yanillian hops in a hops patch
17
  • Raise and breed Rabbits in small animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Access the Player-owned farm (with 20 Construction)
  • One of the requirements for Forgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf
18
  • Plant Valerian seeds and grow Valerian in Daemonheim
19
  • Plant Tarromin seeds and grow Tarromin in a herb patch
20
  • Plant Sweetcorn seeds and grow Sweetcorn in an allotment patch
  • Pick Blightleaf in Daemonheim
21
  • Plant Krandorian seeds and grow Krandorian hops in a hops patch
22
  • Plant Cadavaberry seeds and grow Cadava berries in a bush patch
  • Nurture Evil maple tree saplings
23
  • Construct and erect Scarecrows in a flower patch
  • One of the requirements for the Falador medium tasks
24
  • Plant Nasturtium seeds and grow Nasturtium in a flower patch
25
  • Plant Woad seeds and grow Woad leaves in a flower patch
  • Use the Scroll of life (with 25 Dungeoneering)
  • One of the requirements for Garden of Tranquillity
26
  • Plant Limpwurt seeds and grow Limpwurt roots in a flower patch
  • Plant Harralander seeds and grow Harralander in a herb patch
  • One of the requirements for Spirit of Summer
  • One of the requirements for the Morytania easy tasks
27
  • Plant Apple tree seeds and grow Apple trees in a tree patch
  • One of the requirements for the Karamja medium tasks
28
  • Plant Wildblood seeds and grow Wildblood hops in a hops patch
  • Raise and breed Chickens in small animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Build a second small animal pen at the Player-owned farm (with 20 Construction)
29
  • Plant Gout tubers and grow Goutweed in a herb patch (after Eadgar's Ruse)
  • Plant Aloe seeds and grow Aloe in Daemonheim
  • One of the requirements for My Arm's Big Adventure
30
  • Plant Willow seeds and grow willow trees in a tree patch
  • Nurture Evil yew tree saplings
  • Pick Roseblood in Daemonheim
  • Cultivate Rose bushes in Het's Oasis
  • One of the requirements for Enlightened Journey
  • One of the requirements for the Varrock medium tasks

  • Plant Strawberry seeds and grow Strawberries in an allotment patch

  • Plant Ranarr seeds and grow Ranarr in a herb patch
33
  • Plant Banana tree seeds and grow Banana trees in a fruit tree patch
34
  • Plant Gissel mushroom spores and grow Gissel mushrooms in Daemonheim
35
  • Raise and breed Sheep in medium animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Build a medium animal pen at the Player-owned farm (with 40 Construction)
36
  • Plant Dwellberry seeds and grow Dwellberries in a bush patch
  • Plant Spirit weed seeds and grow Spirit weed in a herb patch
37
  • Nurture Evil magic tree saplings
38
  • Plant Toadflax seeds and grow Toadflax in a herb patch

  • Plant Orange tree seeds and grow Orange trees in a fruit tree patch
40
  • Plant Wormwood seeds and grow Wormwood in Daemonheim
  • Pick Bryll in Daemonheim
  • One of the requirements for Rum Deal
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim medium tasks
41
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
42
  • Plant Curry tree seeds and grow Curry trees in a fruit tree patch
  • Plant Evil turnip seeds and grow Evil turnips in their own patch
  • Nurture Evil elder tree saplings
  • Raise and breed Frogs on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Build a small dino pen at the Ranch Out of Time (with 45 Construction)
43
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
44
  • Plant Irit seeds and grow Irit in a herb patch
45
  • Plant Maple seeds and grow Maple trees in a tree patch
  • One of the requirements for Grim Tales

  • Plant Wergali seeds and grow Wergali in Herblore Habitat
47
  • Plant Watermelon seeds and grow Watermelons in an allotment patch
  • One of the requirements for the Ardougne medium tasks
48
  • Plant Jangerberry seeds and grow Jangerberries in a bush patch
49
  • Raise and breed Cows in large animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Build a large animal pen at the Player-owned farm (with 60 Construction)
  • One of the requirements for A Fairy Tale II - Cure a Queen
50
  • Plant Avantoe seeds and grow Avantoe in a herb patch
  • Pick duskweed in Daemonheim
  • Cultivate Iris bushes in Het's Oasis
  • Ability to help Polletix at the Garden of Kharid
  • Ability to purchase Plant Power (tier 1) at Sydekix's Shop of Balance
51
  • Plant Pineapple seeds and grow Pineapple plants in a fruit tree patch
  • Plant Magebane seeds and grow Magebane in Daemonheim
52
  • Plant White lily seeds and grow White lilies in a flower patch
53
  • Plant Bittercap mushroom spores and grow Bittercap mushrooms in the Mushroom patch
  • Plant the Jade vine seed and grow the Jade vine in the Handelmort Mansion patch in East Ardougne (after Back to my Roots)
  • One of the requirements for Back to my Roots
  • One of the requirements for the Morytania medium tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Ardougne hard tasks
54
  • Plant Red, Blue, and Green blossom seeds, and grow Red, Blue, and Green vine blossoms in Herblore Habitat
  • Raise and breed Chinchompas in small animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • One of the requirements for A Fairy Tale III - Battle at Ork's Rift
55
  • Plant Cactus seeds and grow Cacti in the cactus patches
  • One of the requirements for the Desert hard tasks
56
  • Plant Kwuarm seeds and grow Kwuarm in a herb patch
57
  • Plant Papaya tree seeds and grow Papaya trees in a fruit tree patch
  • Plant Bloodweed seeds and grow Bloodweed in a herb patch
  • One of the requirements for the Ardougne hard tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Tirannwn medium tasks
58
  • Plant Erzille seeds and grow Erzille in Herblore Habitat
59
  • Plant Whiteberry seeds and grow White berries in a bush patch
60
  • Plant Yew seeds and grow Yew trees in a tree patch
  • Pick Soulbell in Daemonheim
  • Access Livid Farm (after Lunar Diplomacy and with 70 Magic, 60 Agility, 60 Crafting, and 50 Construction)
  • One of the requirements for the Falador hard tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Fremennik Province hard tasks
61
  • Plant Lergberry seeds and grow Lergberry bushes in Herblore Habitat
62
  • Plant Snapdragon seeds and grow Snapdragon in a herb patch
  • Plant Featherfoil seeds and grow Featherfoil in Daemonheim
63
  • Plant Cave nightshade seeds and grow cave nightshade in their own patch
  • One of the requirements for The Branches of Darkmeyer
64
  • Raise and breed Spiders in medium animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Build a second medium animal pen at the Player-owned farm (with 40 Construction)
65
  • Plant Argway seeds and grow Argway in Herblore Habitat
  • One of the requirements for While Guthix Sleeps
66
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
67
  • Plant Cadantine seeds and grow Cadantine in a herb patch
68
  • Plant Palm tree seeds and grow Palm trees in a fruit tree patch
  • Plant Edicap mushroom spores and grow Edicap mushrooms in Daemonheim
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim hard tasks
69
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
70
  • Plant Poison ivy seeds and grow Poison ivy berries in a bush patch
  • Plant Ugune seeds and grow Ugune in Herblore Habitat
  • Pick Ectograss in Daemonheim
  • Collect Master farmer fragments while training
  • Plant Devil's snare seed on Anachronia
  • Cultivate Hydrangea bushes in Het's Oasis
  • One of the requirements for the Varrock hard tasks
  • Ability to purchase Plant Power (tier 2) at Sydekix's Shop of Balance
71
  • Raise and breed Yaks in large animal pens at the Player-owned farm
  • Build a second large animal pen at the Player-owned farm (with 60 Construction)
72
  • Plant Calquat tree seeds and grow Calquat trees in their own patch (after Jungle Potion)
73
  • Plant Lantadyme seeds and grow Lantadyme in a herb patch
  • Plant Winter's grip seeds and grow Winter's grip in Daemonheim
74
  • Plant Morchella mushroom spores and grow Morchella mushrooms in the mushroom patch
75
  • Plant Magic seeds and grow Magic trees in a tree patch
  • Plant Harmony moss seeds and grow Harmony moss at Harmony pillars in Prifddinas
  • Plant Luminous snaggler seed on Anachronia
  • One of the requirements for the Falador elite tasks
76
  • Plant Shengo seeds and grow Shengo in Herblore Habitat
  • Plant Prickly pear seeds in the Cactus patches (picking awards Defence experience)
  • Raise and breed Jadinkos on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Build a medium dino pen on the Ranch Out of Time (with 80 Construction)
77
  • Plant Kalferberry seeds and grow Kalferberries in Herblore Habitat
  • Plant Barberry seeds and grow Barberry bushes in a bush patch (picking awards Agility experience)
  • Plant Arbuck seeds and grow Arbuck in a herb patch
78
  • Plant Reed seeds and produce Swamp toads, Swamp tar, and/or Green salamanders in a hops patch
79
  • Plant Dwarf weed seeds and grow Dwarf weed in a herb patch
80
  • Plant Samaden seeds and grow Samaden in Herblore Habitat
  • Add Polypore spores and fire runes to Polypore sticks to produce Polypore staves
  • Pick Runeleaf in Daemonheim
  • Plant Snape grass seeds and grow Snape grass in an allotment patch
  • Create Master farmer outfit pieces (with 20 Invention after discovering the blueprints)
81
  • Raise and breed Zygomites in medium animal pens at the Player-owned farm
82
  • Plant Lampenflora seed on Anachronia
83
  • Plant Spirit tree seeds and grow up to one Spirit tree at a time in a spirit tree patch
84
  • Plant Lycopus seeds and grow Lycopus in Daemonheim
85
  • Plant Torstol seeds and grow Torstol in a herb patch
86
  • Plant an additional Spirit tree seed in a spirit tree patch, for a total of two at a time (after The Prisoner of Glouphrie)
  • Plant Potato cactus seeds and grow Potato cacti in the cactus patches
  • Pick Rumberries from Rumberry bushes on Tuai Leit
  • Pick Exuberries from Exuberry bushes on Uncharted Islands
87
  • Plant Sunchoke seeds in an allotment patch (picking awards Strength experience)
88
  • Plant Butterfly flower seeds in a flower patch for Ruby Harvests
89
  • Plant an additional Spirit tree seed in a spirit tree patch, for a total of three at a time (after The Prisoner of Glouphrie and Plague's End)
90
  • Pick Spiritbloom in Daemonheim
  • Plant Elder seeds and grow Elder trees in a tree patch
  • Pick from Mushroom clusters on Goshima and The Islands That Once Were Turtles
  • Plant Liverwort seed on Anachronia
  • Cultivate Hollyhock bushes in Het's Oasis
  • One of the requirements for the Tirannwn elite tasks
  • Ability to purchase Plant Power (tier 3) at Sydekix's Shop of Balance
91
  • Plant Fellstalk seeds and grow Fellstalk in a herb patch
  • Nourish Starved ancient effigies in Farming
  • One of the requirements for the Morytania elite tasks
92
  • Raise and breed Dragons in large animal pens at the Player-owned farm
93
  • Nourish Nourished ancient effigies in Farming
  • Plant Fly trap seeds in a allotment patch (picking awards Thieving experience)
94
  • Plant a Crystal acorn to grow Crystal trees south east of Prifddinas lodestone
  • Pick from Mushroom clusters on Uncharted islands
95
  • Nourish Sated ancient effigies in Farming
  • Plant Buckthorn seeds and grow Buckthorn in Daemonheim
  • Plant Grapevine seeds in a hops patch
  • Plant Dragonfruit seeds and grow Dragonfruits in the Cactus patches
96
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
97
  • Nourish Gorged ancient effigies in Farming
  • Raise and breed Varanusaurs on The Ranch Out of Time
98
  • Raise and breed Arcane apoterrasaurs on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Build the first large dino pen on the Ranch Out of Time (with 90 Construction)
99
  • Skill mastery
    • Ability to purchase and equip the Farming cape and hood from Martin the Master Gardener in Draynor Village
  • Collect Master farmer fragments at double the rate
  • The ability to obtain Money tree seeds
  • Plant Avocado seeds and grow Avocados in a bush patch
100
  • Raise and breed Brutish Dinosaurs on The Ranch Out of Time
101
  • Plant Ciku seeds and grow Ciku trees in a fruit tree patch
102
  • Raise and breed Salamanders on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Double yield from harvesting Marigolds and Nasturtiums
103
  • Plant Stinkshroom spores and grow Stinkshrooms in the mushroom patch
104
  • Raise and breed Scimitops on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Build the second large dino pen on the Ranch out of Time (with 90 Construction)
105
  • Plant Mango seeds and grow Mangos in a bush patch
106
  • Raise and breed Bagrada Rexes on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Triple yield from harvesting Marigolds and Nasturtiums
107
  • Plant Guarana seeds and grow Guarana trees in a fruit tree patch
108
  • Raise and breed Spicati apoterrasaurs on The Ranch Out of Time
109
  • Plant Tombshroom spores and grow Tombshrooms in the Mushroom patch
110
  • Raise and breed Asciatops on The Ranch Out of Time
  • Quadruple yield from harvesting Marigolds and Nasturtiums
  • Ability to purchase Plant Power (tier 4) at Sydekix's Shop of Balance
111
  • Plant Lychee seeds and grow Lychees in a bush patch
112
  • Raise and breed Corbicula Rexes on The Ranch Out of Time
113
  • Plant Carambola seeds and grow Carambola trees in a fruit tree patch
114
  • Plant a 4th Spirit tree (after reaching Apprentice Farmer rank in the Farming Guild
  • Quintuple yield from harvesting Marigolds and Nasturtiums
115
  • Raise and breed Oculi apoterrasaurs on The Ranch Out of Time
116
  • Plant Golden dragonfruit seeds and grow Golden dragonfruit in the Cactus patch
117
  • Raise and breed Malletops on The Ranch Out of Time
118
  • Raise and breed Pavosaurus rexes on The Ranch Out of Time
119

120
  • True Skill Mastery
  • Ability to purchase and equip the Farming master cape from Martin the Master Gardener in Draynor Village
  • Collect Master farmer fragments at triple the rate
