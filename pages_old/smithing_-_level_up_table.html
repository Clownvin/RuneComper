LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Increases the chance of getting the Smithy pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level in Smithing slightly increases the experience earned from Clan Citadel furnaces
  • Each level in Smithing decreases the cost required to repair various weapons & armour on an armour stand in a player-owned house by 0.5%
  • Each level in Smithing increases the damage from armour spikes by 1 LP.
