  • update 26 June 2023 (Update):
    • 19 achievements which were required for both the master quest cape and the trimmed completionist cape have been removed as requirement from the latter as the latter now requires the former. These achievements were: Advanced Sweeping, All Rise, Around the World in Six Ways, Banking History, Complete Trekky, Count All Light Fingers, Eastern Adventurer, EEEEAGLE!, Ice Ice Chimpy, Ivan is Flailing, Milk of Chocolate, Snape of Grass, Mysteries - Guild, Quiet But Deadly Lore, Research - Guild, Scabigail, Scribbling in the Depths, White Knight Rises, and You're Not My Real Mah.
