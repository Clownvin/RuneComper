Salty is an achievement that requires the player to complete most content in The Arc.

Completion of this achievement unlocks the Salty [Name] title.

Requirements[edit | edit source]

Arc Journal completion and rewards:
Complete all sections of the Arc Journal
Claimed the Farming XP lamp from Ani the farming merchant on Tuai Leit for having discovered and grown all berries
Claimed Iago from Odd Billy on Cyclosis after having found all birds of the Arc
Claimed the kami reward from Sensei Seaworth for finding all bakami and orokami
Convinced Flippers McGraw to join your crew

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • update 2 November 2020 (Update):
    • It was renamed from Arc - Salty to Salty.
  • update 24 June 2019 (Update):
    • Moved the achievement from the Exploration achievements to the Trimmed Completionist Cape subcategory of Completionist achievements.
  • patch 10 September 2018 (Update):
    • The Salty title requirements listed in the achievement interface now correctly includes activating all the Arc tortle portals.
  • hidden 18 June 2018 (Update):
    • Moved the achievement from Miscellaneous achievements to Exploration achievements.
  • update 4 December 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Between 24 June and 24 July 2019, this achievement had a grace period meaning that they were technically a requirement for the trimmed completionist cape, but did not count as such until the grace period ended after which the requirement was actually required.
