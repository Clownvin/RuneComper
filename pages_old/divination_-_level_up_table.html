LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level gained in Divination slightly increases a player's chance of successfully harvesting divine energy
  • Each level increases experience gained from cursed wisps and cursed energy
  • Experience for depositing Chronicle fragments is increased
  • Increases the chance of getting the Willow pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level increases the amount of energy obtained from the nightmare muspah's special move
