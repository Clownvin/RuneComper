Aura Aura Aura! is an achievement that requires the player to unlock every aura available from War's Retreat. This requires 92,500 Marks of War.

Unlock/item Marks of War Description
Vampyrism aura 5,000 Gain life points equal to 5% of any damage you deal.
Dark magic aura 12,500 When attacking you have a chance to inflict corrupting damage on an opponent.
Berserker aura 25,000 Boosts your Attack and Strength by 10% but reduces your Defence by 15%. Does not work in the Wilderness.
Reckless aura 25,000 Boosts your Ranged by 10% but reduces your Defence by 15%. Does not work in the Wilderness.
Maniacal aura 25,000 Boosts your Magic by 10% but reduces your Defence by 15%. Does not work in the Wilderness.

Update history[edit | edit source]

  • patch 20 July 2020 (Update):
    • Some of the other achievements from War's Retreat have been shifted around and had various adjustments to their Runescore, but the total amount of Runescore obtained from all of these achievement remains the same. This achievement kept 30 RuneScore and was not changed.
  • update 27 January 2020 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement's name is a reference to JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, in particular to Star Platinum's battlecry of "Ora Ora Ora".[1]
