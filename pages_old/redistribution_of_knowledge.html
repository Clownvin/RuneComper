Redistribution of knowledge is an achievement which requires the player to claim the Thieving XP lamp from Caelyn Kadaan after the Heartstealer quest.

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • update 8 February 2021 (Update):
    • Added to game.
      • Heartstealer quest released.
      • If you have at least level 20 Thieving you'll also be able to claim a Thieving XP lamp from Caelyn after the quest.
