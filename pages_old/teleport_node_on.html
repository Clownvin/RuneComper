Teleport Node On is a mystery and achievement, which enables players to activate the entire dragonkin teleportation network on Anachronia, by activating all the Orthen teleportation devices across the island. When unlocking the portals, be sure to visit them in the order listed below, as each one unlocks the special research required to activate the next.

Steps[edit | edit source]

Crypt of Varanus[edit | edit source]

Crypt of Varanus must be activated first, and each subsequent portal must be activated in the order below, as each portal unlocks the special research required for activation of the next portal. To activate teleportation to the first Orthen Dig Site location:

  • Follow the map to get to the Crypt of Varanus.
  • Click on the inactive teleportation device to activate it, rewarding 3,000 experience
  • You can later use the Orthen teleportation device at the base camp to quickly return here.

Observation outpost[edit | edit source]

To activate teleportation to the second Orthen Dig Site location:

  • Complete the Incomplete Portal Network I special research at the Exam Centre in the Archaeology Campus.
  • Withdraw 20 unnoted Ancient vis. Noted ancient vis doesn't work.
  • Head to the observation outpost by following the map.
  • Click on the inactive teleportation device at the outpost with 20 unnoted ancient vis in backpack to activate it, rewarding 3,000 experience. This requires level 70 Runecrafting.

Moksha ritual site[edit | edit source]

To activate teleportation to the third Orthen Dig Site location:

  • Start the Incomplete Portal Network II special research.
  • Follow the Know Thy Measure guide to unlock the Moksha ritual site.
  • View the report of the special research if you have not done it already (wait until it is complete).
  • Return to the Moksha ritual site with 20 unnoted Orthenglass. Noted orthenglass doesn't work.
  • Inside, east of the exit is the inactive teleportation device.
  • Repair it using the orthenglass, rewarding 3,000 experience. This requires level 70 Crafting.

Xolo city[edit | edit source]

To activate teleportation to the fourth Orthen Dig Site location:

  • Complete the Incomplete Portal Network III special research.
  • View the report of the special research if you have not done it already (wait until it is complete).
  • Withdraw 20 unnoted Quintessence and carry it with you. Noted quintessence doesn't work.
  • Head to the south-eastern tip of Anachronia.
    • Use the teleportation device in the base camp to Moksha ritual site, and continue to follow the map to get to the xolo city location.
    • Slayer skillcape provides the fastest method to get there with the "Laniakea" option.
  • North of Laniakea and west of the bottom of the stairs you'll find the last inactive teleportation device.
  • Activate it with the quintessence, rewarding 3,000 experience. This requires level 70 Divination.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The player Sean was the first player who completed this mystery on 19 October 2020 which was shown in-game with the announcement: News: Sean is the first to solve the 'Teleport Node On' mystery!
  • The name of this mystery is a reference to the Nodon dragonkin, who were primarily builders and architects.
