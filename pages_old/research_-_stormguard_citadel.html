Research - Stormguard Citadel is an achievement that requires the player to complete all the special research related to the Stormguard Citadel Dig Site listed below.

This achievement had a grace period which ended on 30 September 2020.

Research[edit | edit source]

Sequence number[a 1] Mission name Level Where to start Base cost (chronotes) Rewards
1 Ikovian Tragedy 70 Study the Ikovian temple by clicking either the northern or southern archway of the Temple of Ikov. 233
  • 6,666.1−6,666.9 experience
  • 1x Ikovian gerege (damaged)
2 Deer-ly Departed 81 Study the memorial megalith in the Stormguard Citadel - Keshik memorial excavation site. 266
  • 10,555 experience
  • 1x Aviansie dreamcoat (damaged)
3 The Aetherium Giant 98 Study the war golem arm in the middle of the south-western islands of Stormguard Citadel Dig Site. You'll need to be in flight, wearing the wingsuit v2 or higher to be able to reach this location. 326
  • 23,809.1−23,809.9 experience
  • 1x Golem instruction (damaged)
4 Night and Day 95 Study the keshik coat of arms at the Nightguard Tower (north-western island). You'll need the wingsuit v2 or higher to be able to reach this location. 313
  • 20,238 experience
  • 1x Stormguard gerege (damaged)
5 Howling Man 118 Study the theoretical blueprints in Howl's Floating Workshop. You'll need a Stormguard gerege in your tool belt to access this area. 393
  • 61,904.1−61,904.9 experience
  • 1x Chuluu stone (damaged)
6 Back to the Drawing Board 95 Study and search (right-click) the drafting bench inside Howl's Floating Workshop. You'll need a Stormguard gerege in your tool belt to access this area. 313
  • 20,238 experience
  • 1x Prototype godsword (damaged)
  • 50x Torn blueprint fragments (Howl's workshop)
