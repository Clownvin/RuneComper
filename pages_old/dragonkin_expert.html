Dragonkin Expert is an achievement that requires the player to have restored all 22 dragonkin artefacts from the Orthen Dig Site. There are seven artefacts in Dragonkin I, four artefacts in Dragonkin II, five artefacts in Dragonkin III, and six artefacts in Dragonkin IV.

Completing this achievement rewards the player with the title, Dragonkin Expert [Name].
