Fremennik Set Tasks - Medium
Release date 18 April 2017 (Update)
Members Yes
Description Given by Yrsa in Rellekka for completing all Medium Tasks in the Fremennik Province.
RuneScore 10
Category Area Tasks
Rewards
  • Fremennik sea boots 2
  • Magic lamp worth 10,000xp in a skill of your choice.
  • The boots gain the following extra benefits:
    • One chargeless teleport with the lyre per day.
    • Shortcut between the piers of Etceteria and Miscellania.
  • Your kingdom's approval rating will increase faster.
Requirements
  • See requirements.

Fremennik Set Tasks - Medium is an achievement that is rewarded upon finishing the Medium Fremennik achievements.
