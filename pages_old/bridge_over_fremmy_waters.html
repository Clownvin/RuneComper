Bridge over Fremmy Waters is an achievement that requires the player to finish all five unabridged Fremennik Sagas.

Sagas to complete
Three's Company (saga)
Vengeance (saga)
Thok It To 'Em (saga)
Nadir (saga)
Thok Your Block Off (saga)

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement is identical in function to the Daemonheim achievement: Epic Epic.
    • Unlike the Epic Epic achievement, completing Three's Company (saga) unabridged on a free-to-play world will tick it off from the list.
  • The name of the achievement is a reference to the song Bridge over Troubled Water.
