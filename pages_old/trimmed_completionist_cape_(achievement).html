Trimmed Completionist Cape is an achievement that requires the player to complete all the following achievements, awarding the player the ability to claim a trimmed completionist cape.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be includedsee here for how to help out!
  • patch 17 April 2023 (Update):
    • Penance to the King and Walk on the Wildy Side III removed from requirements.
    • A Druid's Sidekick, Build Them All!, Conquered Everyone's Heart, Draught Includer, Get Tooled Up, Granny's Favourite Farmer, My Goebie Homies, Relic-weary, Sort of Crystally, Scrolling in the Deep, and The Golden Crocodile Hunter added to requirements, with Scrolling in the Deep being on grace period until 15 April 2024.
  • update 13 September 2021 (Update):
    • The Conquest Adore achievement is no longer required for the Trimmed Completionist Cape.
  • update 18 April 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Before an update on 10 September 2018, this achievement had 75 RuneScore.
