Steps[edit | edit source]

Locations of the scaffolding and table.
  • Obtain two mystery pages randomly while excavating from the following spots:
    • A warning letter, from marketplace debris (requires level 63 Archaeology).
    • A scrawled note, from gladiator remains (requires level 66 Archaeology).
  • In a house at the far south end of the Nodon Front, east of the house with Naressa, study the table to receive a badly written message and unlock the Complete the Pattern, Solve the Puzzle research.
  • Complete the research to receive the message in the box. This is performed at the Exam Centre.
  • Talk to Jimmy. He is located where you teleport into the Senntisten Digsite.
  • Obtain a bloodstained note while excavating at marketplace debris (requires level 63 Archaeology).
  • After finding all the above pages, find the scaffolding in the southwest corner of Senntisten and select the "squeeze through" option.
  • Investigate the Investigator's notes under the chair in the house to receive Investigator's note, solving the mystery.
