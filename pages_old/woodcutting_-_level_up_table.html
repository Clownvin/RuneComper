LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Woodcutting slightly increases a player's chance of successfully chopping trees
  • Increases the chance of getting the Woody pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level in Woodcutting slightly increases the experience earned at Clan Citadel trees
