LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Agility allows you to increase your run energy faster and decrease your run energy slower as well as decreasing your chance of failing obstacles (including at shortcuts)
  • Increases the XP gained from the Wilderness Agility Course with the demonic skull equipped, starting at level 50
  • Increases the chance of getting the Dojo Mojo pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
