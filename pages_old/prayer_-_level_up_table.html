LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Prayer adds an additional 10 points to a player's pool of prayer points
  • Each level in Prayer from 46+ increases the damage Retribution and Wrath do to the enemy
  • Each level in Prayer from 49+ increases the amount you're healed from Redemption
  • Increases the damage from the Ancestor spirits aura's effect
