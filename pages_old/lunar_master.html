Lunar Master is an achievement that tracks the player's progress into unlocking the eleven Livid Farm spells.

Players can quickly progress this task by purchasing Livid plants from the Deep Sea Fishing Merchant, or by completing one of Pauline Polaris's medium requests at Manor Farm.

Though it is not clearly specified, the spells must be purchased in order. The "produce points" indicated in the top right of Pauline's "Rewards" tab incorrectly states the amount of points you have, as the proper amount of Produce points you have will be correctly displayed in your currency pouch. The Produce Points Pauline lists are the total cumulative points you have earned throughout your playtime of the game.

Each purchased Livid plant grants 10,000–40,000 produce points, thus if only purchased plants are used, it will take around 17 livid plants worth 17,000,000 to purchase the 430,000 points required to complete this achievement (assuming 25K average points per plant).
