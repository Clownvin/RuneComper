Elven Titles is an achievement that requires the player to obtain the clan-named elven titles after Plague's End in Prifddinas. You can see your progress on any of the titles by visiting the Customisations interface and looking at the Titles tab. The Elven titles are labeled under Miscellaneous Titles. Make sure that you have disabled filtering in the bottom left of the window in order to show titles you have not yet unlocked.

Title Task
[Name] of the Trahaearn Obtain 100 corrupted ores while mining Seren stones.
[Name] of the Iorwerth Complete 8 dark beast slayer assignments assigned by Morvran.
[Name] of the Cadarn Defeat a total of 1,000 Cadarn rangers or magi in combat.
[Name] of the Crwys Check the health of an elder tree grown in the elder tree patch in the Crwys Clan district in Prifddinas.
[Name] of the Amlodd Summon a light creature familiar, use a Light Creature scroll (Enlightenment), then talk to Lord Amlodd.
[Name] of the Ithell Craft 100 crystal flasks.
[Name] of the Hefin Complete 200 laps of the Hefin Agility Course.
[Name] of the Meilyr Harvest 50 harmony moss from the Harmony pillars.
