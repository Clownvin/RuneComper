Overview[edit | edit source]

Alignment Saradominist
Dig site Everlight Dig Site
Requirements
  • 105 Archaeology [B]
Required items These items can be obtained before and/or during the mystery.
  • Dominion torch (will be lost)
  • 'Hallowed Be the Everlight' painting
  • 300 mosaic pieces
Rewards 49,000 experience

Steps[edit | edit source]

Restoring a damaged mosaic tile
The top floor of the lighthouse
  • Enter the door under the Acropolis in the south of Everlight by interacting with the sconce with a Dominion torch in your inventory (level 69 Archaeology required).
    • A damaged dominion torch can be obtained through either the Stadio Ga Ga research or it can be obtained from the Dominion games podium excavation site.
  • Light the torch.
  • After the door unseals, enter the underwater cave.
  • Walk west through the tunnel. A message will appear saying that the mystery has been unlocked. Traverse the cave in to the north and continue until you reach the rubble.
  • Skip over the Rubble to enter the most northern area, then go diving in the water to the east of the damaged mosaics. Be sure both hands are free. A diving outfit is not required (although wearing one unlocks the A Little Overdressed achievement).
  • Find the three cogs (north, west, and south) and place them in the three slots on the ground to fix the broken mechanisms (you can only carry them one by one). See the map below.
  • Next, return to the surface using the bubbles and repair the damaged mosaics by using the 'Hallowed Be the Everlight' painting and 300 mosaic pieces. This will not consume the painting.
    • Mosaic pieces can be obtained from excavating the stockpiled art excavation hotspot (requiring level 105 Archaeology). 1-10 pieces are dropped randomly at a time.
  • After repairing the mosaic, activate the lighthouse elevator by clicking on the Lighthouse elevator in the centre of the mosaic. Go up to solve the mystery.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name of this mystery may refer to the second line of the Lord's Prayer.
  • The player Roskat was the first player who completed this mystery on 5 April 2020 which was shown in-game with the announcement: News: Roskat is the first to solve the 'Hallowed Be...' mystery!
