A wingsuit v1 is an artefact made at an archaeologist's workbench with level 76 Archaeology and level 40 Smithing, though it can be restored at level 73 Archaeology with an archaeology potion.

It can also be restored at level 70 Archaeology, when hit by an advanced pulse core. However the boost to level 76 is limited to only 1 minute and decreases in time. Since it's nearly impossible to use the minute to teleport to the Stormguard Citadel Dig Site (in order to obtain the needed Armadylean yellow), it's advised to buy this on the Grand Exchange, before restoring the artefact.

The wingsuit v1 (damaged) can be found in the building just north of the excavation hotspot keshik ger.

The wingsuit can be upgraded to wingsuit v2 and subsequently to wingsuit v3 by finding the four upgrades, and navigating the Stormguard Citadel Dig Site. The wings can be added to the tool belt.

Restoration[edit | edit source]

Wingsuit v1 on the ground
Wingsuit v1 (damaged) location
  • Navigate to the first island containing the keshik ger excavation hotspots.
  • Enter the broken-down building just north-east of the spots.
  • Click on the Wingsuit visible on the ground to get the wingsuit v1 (damaged).
  • Repair the wingsuit at a restoration station.
  • Add it to your tool belt.
