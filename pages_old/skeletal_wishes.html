Skeletal Wishes is an achievement that requires the player to complete the Rag and Bone Man wish list and the Fur 'n' Seek wish list, and then having the Odd Old Man rebuild the skeletal horror. The rebuilding objective can only be completed after the skeletal horror has been defeated once.

Objectives
Rag and Bone Man wish list
Fur 'n' Seek wish list
Rebuild the skeletal horror

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Prior to the update on 8 April 2019, the description of the achievement was "Complete Rag and Bone Man and Fur 'n Seek wishlists, and have the Odd Old Man rebuild the Skeletal Horror."
