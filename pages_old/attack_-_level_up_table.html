LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Attack contributes to a player's combat level
  • Each level in Attack slightly increases the accuracy of a player's melee attacks
  • Each level in Attack slightly increases ability damage when fighting demonic creatures with Silverlight and Darklight
