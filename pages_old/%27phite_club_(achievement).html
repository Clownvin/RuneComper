'Phite Club is an achievement that requires the player to complete the 'Phite Club quest.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • update 2 November 2020 (Update):
    • This achievement had its RuneScore reduced from 40 to 0.
  • update 5 June 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The quest's name (and consequently this achievement's name) is a reference to the 1999 film Fight Club.
