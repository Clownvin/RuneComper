Benedict's World Tour (miniquest) is a miniquest for promotion of the NXT client. It is available to both free-to-play and members. The first nine locations are available to all players, and the last seven locations are exclusive to members.

Starting out[edit | edit source]

The miniquest can be started by talking to Benedict, who can be found in the building near Charlie the Tramp, just north-west of the Varrock lodestone. Benedict will ask the player to help find his lost items across Gielinor. He will give the player a scrapbook, which provides an image clue for each item.

To complete the miniquest, the player has to find each item. Each item has a loot beam shining over it and will show up as a red dot on the minimap, even if they are on a different floor than you. They can be found in locations that show-off the benefits of the NXT client. Upon collecting the item, the player is shown a short, unskippable cutscene.

Return to Benedict for the reward.

A different order to things[edit | edit source]

It is recommended to do items out of order, as their chronological order isn't the most efficient way to do them.

  • First, after initiating the miniquest, the player can travel to Varrock Palace to collect the fox pelt. Mark is located at the top floor of the North-West tower.
  • Next, the Al Kharid Palace and Het's Oasis items can be done relatively quickly.
    • Partial completion of Stolen Hearts, up to the point where the roof of Al Kharid Palace is reached is required to find the toffee apple (top of the stairs). This can be done during the quest. The Het's Oasis item is right in front of the Statue of Het. Level 65 Agility is not needed; go north around the whirligig pool to cross the fallen palm tree which has no agility requirement.
  • The White Wolf Mountain and Grand Tree items should be next via the Gnome Glider. At the grand tree climb down the ladder and the beam should be south of the Gnome guards.
  • Take the Lumbridge Lodestone and take the stairs to the top of the castle. The beam is north of the bank, top of the stairs.
  • Travel to Castle Wars, via a ring of duelling. The item is located North-West of the Saradomin portal.
  • Take the hot air balloon to Varrock, or use the Earth Altar teleport (Wicked Hood), the Invitation Box, the Skeletal horror Teleport, or the Fort Forinthry lodestone. Then walk to Paterdomus. The item is near the beacon, south of the temple.
  • Use the Wicked hood to reach the Runespan item (Low level Runespan portal).
  • Then use the nearby fairy ring outside of the Wizard's Tower (or a Portable fairy ring) to complete the Polypore Dungeon item (code BIP) and the Mudskipper Point item (code AIQ), as well as using the Legend's Guild fairy ring (code BLR) to travel to the Sorcerer's Tower top floor item.
  • Take the Bandit Camp, the Al Kharid Lodestone or use the grouping system to teleport to Liberation of Mazcab to tackle the Tuska's Corpse and Mazcab items.
  • Teleport to Falador, either using the Lodestone or the Falador Teleport to obtain the Falador Castle item. It is located on the top floor of the west side of the castle, near Commander Zilyana and the herb patch.
  • Finally, use Ashdale's Lodestone or a Games Necklace to reach Ashdale's item. It is located south of the lodestone, next to Magda.

Achievements[edit | edit source]

  • Benedict's World Tour, Part 1 ( 5) – Find the first 9 items in Benedict's World Tour and receive the XP lamp.
  • Benedict's World Tour (miniquest) ( 5) – Find all locations in Benedict's World Tour and receive the XP lamp.
  • I've Got a Little List (of Miniquests) ( 0) – Complete all miniquests listed in the Quests interface. Does not include sagas.
