Rewards 5,100 experience

Steps[edit | edit source]

  • Gather all five athlete's journal pages from excavation hotspots located in Everlight - Dominion Games stadium excavation site: stadio debris, Dominion Games podium, and icyene weapon rack. Finding the first journal page will start the mystery.
  • Turn all five athlete's journal pages in to Vanescula Drakan to complete the mystery. If the pages have been transcribed to your Archaeology journal, speaking to Vanescula is enough.
