Following In The Footsteps is an achievement that requires the player to participate in the Effigy Incubator encounter 12 times and then talk with Vicendithas to find out the outcome of the continued research. Players can use a Distraction & Diversion reset token (monthly) in order to progress the story a second instalment each month. Additionally if the player has completed Extinction, Vicendithas can "accelerate" his research, costing 50,000 Anachronia base camp resources of each type (300,000 total) to unlock each chapter.

Players do not need to make an effigy during the Effigy Incubator encounter or talk with Vicendithas about his research; participating in the encounter suffices.

Players can check their progress by talking with Vicendithas and use the option "About your research..." and then "[See a previous chapter]" to see how many chapters have been unlocked.

After all the chapters are unlocked, you have to talk to Vicendithas and use the option "About your research..." and then "What have you learned?" to unlock the achievement.

This achievement had a grace period lasting until 1 September 2021 meaning that it was not actually required for the master quest cape until that date.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • patch 17 April 2023 (Update):
    • Added an alternative method to complete "Following in the Footsteps"
      • Vicendithas can now 'accelerate' his research on effigies, but requires a wealth of resources to do so.
      • It will cost 50,000 Base Camp Resources of each type (300,000 total) to unlock each chapter.
      • Players must have completed Extinction.
  • patch 9 November 2020 (Update):
    • Fixed an issue where not completing the 'Following in the Footsteps' Achievement during the grace period would cause the Master Quest Cape progression bar to be empty.
  • update 14 September 2020 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • As the Distraction & Diversion reset token (monthly) could initially only be used to progress the story for the months that the player had missed, the earliest a player could have unlocked all 12 chapters was 1 August 2021.
