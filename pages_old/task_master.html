Task Master is an achievement that tracks the player's progress into completing all Area Tasks achievements.

Completing this achievement unlocks the similarly named Taskmaster emote.

  • The table shows the skill levels required to complete all required Area Tasks achievements. This may not include the levels needed to complete quests that also are required to complete some achievements.
  • A b indicates that a temporary boost may be used to reach that level. A minimum of level 90 is required for all of these skills, regardless of boost.
  • The Assist System cannot be used.
  • Requires either maximum quest points, or 1 skill at level 99 and 270 quest points.
  • Though 100 combat is the highest actual requirement, the minimum required combat skills would yield a combat level of 127.

The current XP required to complete all of the achievements is 175,792,057, excluding skill requirements of quests required for some achievements and assuming no skill boosts are used. This is roughly 4.5 times the amount of experience required to complete all current quests and earn the Quest Cape.
