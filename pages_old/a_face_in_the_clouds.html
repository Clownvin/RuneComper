A Face in the Clouds is an achievement that requires the player to bring bacon mound, bread, and a chocolate bar to Seren, who is located upstairs in the Tower of Voices in Prifddinas, and use one of the items on Seren. This also unlocks the title the Elfborne.

    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement used to be a task prior to the release of the Achievements System on 18 April 2017.
  • After completion of Extinction, this achievement can be completed by recalling a projection of Seren by using the memoriam device.
