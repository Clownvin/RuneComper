Sandy is an achievement that requires the player to unlock all Menaphos rewards.

Once the below requirements are completed, talk to Urluk the camel in the Merchant district in Menaphos to unlock the Sandy [Name] title.

Requirements[edit | edit source]

Achievement Notes
  • Reputation - Class Crown ( 40) – Earn tier 10 reputation with the imperial faction in Menaphos.
  • Reputation - Lock, Stock and Barrel ( 40) – Earn tier 10 reputation with the merchant faction in Menaphos.
  • Reputation - Seagullible ( 40) – Earn tier 10 reputation with the ports faction in Menaphos.
  • Reputation - To the Bone ( 40) – Earn tier 10 reputation with the worker faction in Menaphos.
 Gain tier 10 reputation in all four districts/factions of Menaphos.
Menaphos Pyramid Scheme ( 50) – Complete various achievements within Menaphos.
  • Gain tier 10 overall reputation in Menaphos (not to be confused with reputation in a faction/district).
  • Complete The Jack of Spades, Crocodile Tears, Our Man in the North, and 'Phite Club quests.
All Abuzz ( 25) – Recover the Grand Library's collection of valuable and unique insects. Collect all Insects of the Desert for the Menaphos journal.
Feline Good ( 25) – Restore the Grand Library's register of notable cat citizens of Menaphos. Collect all Cats of Menaphos for the Menaphos journal.
Rock and Roll ( 25) – Recover the Grand Library's collection of valuable and unique gemstones. Collect all Jewels of the Elid for the Menaphos journal.
Jack of all Menaphos ( 0) – Do a bit of everything in Menaphos and claim the cape of the palapa from Urluk. Obtain the Palapa Cape override from Urluk.
Gotta Catch 'Em Both! ( 0) – Find the hidden pets in Menaphos. Unlock the Ishhara and Squeck pets.
Follow the Gold-Brick Road ( 0) – Solve the mystery of the Golden Path. Complete the Golden Path Challenge to unlock the basic magic carpet pet.[notes 1]
  1. ^ Although the competition has been removed, the steps from the Golden Path Challenge can still be completed to get the basic magic carpet pet.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • update 2 November 2020 (Update):
    • The achievement was renamed from Meta-phos to Sandy.
  • patch 22 July 2019 (Update):
    • The Meta-Phos "Sandy" title achievement now checks for separate district reputation as opposed to total City reputation.
  • update 24 June 2019 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Between 24 June and 24 July 2019, this achievement had a grace period meaning that they were technically a requirement for the trimmed completionist cape, but did not count as such until the grace period ended after which the requirement was actually required.
