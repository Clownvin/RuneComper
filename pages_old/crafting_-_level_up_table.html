LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Increases the chance of getting the Gemi pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Increases the chance of successfully cutting opal, jade and red topaz
  • Each level gained in Crafting slightly increases the experience earned from Clan Citadel looms
1
  • Craft wizard equipment and leather armour except for the shield and vambraces
  • Craft salve gloves and protoleather vambraces in Daemonheim
  • Craft cracked mining urns, cracked cooking urns, cracked fishing urns, and impious urns
  • Spin balls of wool, balls of black wool, and bowstrings
  • Craft strips of cloth, strips of black cloth, and empty pots
  • Cut uncut opals
  • Craft beer glasses, lapis lazuli brooches, and opal rings
  • Craft the following class 1 items in Stealing Creation: Butterfly nets, harpoons, magic staves, leather bodies, chaps, coifs, mage robe tops, mage robe bottoms, and mage hats
