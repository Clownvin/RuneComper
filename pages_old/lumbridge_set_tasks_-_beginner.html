RuneScore 5
Category Area Tasks
Rewards
  • Explorer's ring 1
  • Magic lamp worth 500xp in a skill of your choice.
  • The ring provides the following benefits:
    • Recharge 50% of your run energy once per day.
    • Slight boost to Prayer and Magic.
    • Access to the Explore emote.

Lumbridge Set Tasks - Beginner is an achievement that is rewarded upon finishing the Beginner Lumbridge achievements.
