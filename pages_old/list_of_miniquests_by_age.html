These are lists of miniquests by age.

RuneScape is currently in the Sixth Age. All Fifth Age miniquests are, narrative-wise, considered to have been completed regardless of whether the player in question has personally completed them. This is reflected in the dialogue and story released since the beginning of the Sixth Age.

There are currently 26 Fifth Age miniquests.
