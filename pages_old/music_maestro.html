Music Maestro is an achievement that tracks the number of music tracks the player has unlocked. The current total is 1,349. However, the following four tracks are not required to complete this achievement:

  • Monkey See, Monkey Do
  • Run, Escape!
  • We Menaphites, We Fight!
  • Welcome To The Easter Fete

For the full list of music tracks, see here.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • patch 12 October 2020 (Update):
    • Players will no longer receive the Music Maestro and Completionist achievements every time they log in or switch worlds.
  • update 24 June 2019 (Update):
    • Music tracks that are locked behind boss encounters will now unlock upon starting the fight.
  • patch 14 January 2019 (Update):
    • Achievement for unlocking all music tracks should now only be completed if all tracks are unlocked.
    • The amount of music tracks required for the Music Maestro achievement has been correct to show the full amount.
  • update 10 September 2018 (Update):
    • Music Maestro achievement now gives 0 RuneScore, down from 50, with the release of RuneScore HiScores.
  • update 18 April 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Before an update on 10 September 2018, this achievement had 50 RuneScore.
  • The music track Family is listed in the achievement's requirements list as "Family of the Elven Queen".
