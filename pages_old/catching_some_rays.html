Catching Some Rays is an achievement that requires one of the following:

For non-ironman:

Catch a raw manta ray in the Fishing Trawler minigame. Fishing level boosts such as fishing potions (+3) and admiral pies (+5) may be used. Boosts can be used right before checking the trawler net as the required level does not have to be met during the activity itself. It is a common misconception that this requires finding a group, but the minigame can be played and the achievement completed solo. Players simply need to complete one round of the minigame with a high fishing level in order to obtain manta rays. Obtaining this fish is not a matter of luck; even catching 1 fish during the minigame will result in the fish being a manta ray provided players have the requisite fishing level.

For ironman:

Successfully cook a rocktail on the Witchaven dock.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement used to be a task prior to the release of the Achievements System on 18 April 2017.
  • The name of this achievement is a pun on the idiom of the same name, which means to sunbathe.
