LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Increases the damage from the Tuska's Wrath ability
  • Increases the chance of getting the Crabbe pet drop, and again at 200 million experience
  • Increases the chance of capturing a soul (from level 99 and above)
1
  • Slay Gelatinous Abominations (with spiked gauntlets)
  • Use enchanted gems
  • Wear spiked gauntlets
  • Receive Slayer assignments from Turael (or Spria during and after While Guthix Sleeps) in Burthorpe
  • Slay icefiends in Daemonheim
  • Ability to raise your Slayer level to 5
  • Receive Slayer assignments from Mazchna (or Achtryn during While Guthix Sleeps) in Canifis (with a combat level of 20)
  • Receive Slayer assignments from Vannaka in Edgeville Dungeon (with a combat level of 40)
  • Receive Slayer assignments from Chaeldar in Zanaris (after Lost City and with a combat level of 70)
  • Receive the Greenhorn daily challenge
  • Ability to raise your Slayer level to 120
