Research - Senntisten is an achievement that requires the player to complete all the research related to the Senntisten Dig Site.

Research[edit | edit source]

Mission name Level Where to start Base cost (chronotes) Rewards
The Hidden Chamber 60 Study the cathedral bookcase found in the northwestern corner of the cathedral at the Senntisten Dig Site. 200
  • 3,333 experience
  • 1x Funerary urn of shadow (damaged)
Prime Real Estate 63 Study the market stall outside of the colosseum gateway at the Senntisten Dig Site. 206
  • 4,333 experience
  • 1x Funerary urn of ice (damaged)
Crossing that Bridge 67 Study the scaffolding north of Jimmy. This requires obtaining the Inquisitior's Memoirs (page 5). See Secrets of the Inquisition for details. 220
  • 5,666 experience
  • 1x Funerary urn of smoke (damaged)
Are You Not Entertained? 66 Study the Gladiator Bones found in the middle of the Colosseum at the Senntisten Dig Site. 220
  • 5,333 experience
  • 1x Funerary urn of blood (damaged)
Complete the Pattern, Solve the Puzzle 63 Study the table in a small house at the far south end of the Nodon Front, south-west from the cathedral and east of the house where Naressa is to unlock the Empty Children mystery and the research. Study the table again if the research is not unlocked, or alternatively talk to Jimmy about the Empty Children mystery. 206
  • 4,333 experience
  • 1x The message in the box
