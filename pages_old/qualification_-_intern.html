Qualification - Intern is an achievement that requires the player to complete the Archaeology tutorial.

Completing this achievement rewards the player with a soil box, access to the Guildmaster's office at the Archaeology Guild, the Intern tab rewards in the Archaeology Reward Shop and the title, [Name] the Archaeology Intern.

After completing the achievement a material manual, a hi-spec monocle, and a tarpaulin sheet may be claimed from Acting Guildmaster Reiniger.
