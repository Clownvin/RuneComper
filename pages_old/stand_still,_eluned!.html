Stand Still, Eluned! is an achievement that requires the player to have Eluned or Ilfeen recharge a crystal teleport seed in the Isafdar forest.

Right-clicking Eluned to recharge does complete the achievement. Selecting the seed and using it on Eluned does not complete the achievement. Recharging a crystal with Ilfeen will also work, but Eluned in Prifddinas will not. Selecting the seed and using it on Ilfeen does work.

The location of Ilfeen is dependent on progress in The Prisoner of Glouphrie quest, if it is completed then one needs to visit Ilfeen in Lletya.

Contacting Islwyn through the NPC Contact spell no longer works for the achievement, but this method can be used to recharge the seed.
