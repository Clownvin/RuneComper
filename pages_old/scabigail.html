Scabigail is an achievement that requires the player to collect all 19 scabarite notes by killing skeletons, dried zombies and scabarites at the northern part of Ullek, or mummies to the west, and adding them to the Scabaras research after Dealing with Scabaras. The completed Scabaras research has to then be shown to Abigail at Ullek ruins.

The last two scabarite notes are not dropped, but found in chests in the dungeon below the plateau. One chest is just to the east of the High Priest of Scabaras's room, while the other chest is further south, surrounded by monsters.

By far the fastest way to get 17 of the 19 notes (18 and 19 are in the chests) is to set up a dwarf multicannon in the dungeon under the plateau, turn auto-retaliate on, and use multi-hitting abilities, such as chain, ricochet, or hurricane. The scarabites here drop the notes rather commonly, whereas the notes seem to be quite rarely dropped from the other sources listed. This task should only take approximately 15 minutes.

If Abigail refuses to give you the reward after getting all the drops and looting the chests, return to the High Priest of Scabaras and talk to him in case you have missed the notes normally given during quest dialogue.
