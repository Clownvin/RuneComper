Reaper Crew is an achievement that requires the player to defeat every listed boss at least once.

Completing this achievement unlocks [Name] the Reaper title and adds the following stats to the player:

  • +2 Prayer bonus
  • +20 Armour
  • +200 life points
  • +12 melee damage bonus
  • +12 ranged damage bonus
  • +12 magic damage bonus

Requirements[edit | edit source]

Level and quest requirements[edit | edit source]

Players need at least the following skills to complete this achievement:

  • 60 Summoning - required to kill Queen Black Dragon.
  • 80 Attack - required to kill Vindicta. [B]
  • 80 Ranged - required to kill Twin Furies. [B]
  • 70 Ranged - required to kill Kree'arra.
  • 80 Magic - required to kill Helwyr. [B]
  • 80 Prayer - required to kill Gregorovic.
  • 70 Strength - required to kill General Graardor.
  • 70 Constitution - required to kill K'ril Tsutsaroth.
  • 70 Agility - required to kill Commander Zilyana.
  • 115 Slayer - required to kill the The Magister. [B]

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • patch 26 June 2023 (Update):
    • Reaper Crew is now a requirement for the Trimmed Completionist Cape.
  • patch 17 January 2022 (Update):
    • The boosts from the Reaper Crew achievement will no longer be applied to players who haven't unlocked it.
  • patch 22 July 2019 (Update):
    • Boss kills prestiged to zero now count towards the Reaper Crew achievement. For realsies this time.
  • patch 8 July 2019 (Update):
    • Fixed a typo within the Reaper Crew achievement which mentioned "200 armour" instead of "20 armour".
  • patch 1 July 2019 (Update):.
    • Boss kills prestiged to zero now count towards the Reaper Crew achievement.
  • update 24 June 2019 (Update):
    • The achievement was moved from the Completionist Cape subcategory of the Completionist achievements to the Boss Kills subcategory of the Combat achievements.
    • The description was changed from "Defeat every boss at least once to earn the title 'the Reaper'." to "Defeat listed bosses at least once."
    • Completing the achievement now adds certain combat bonuses to the account.
  • update 10 September 2018 (Update):
    • The RuneScore for this achievement was reduced from 75 to 0.
  • update 18 April 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.
