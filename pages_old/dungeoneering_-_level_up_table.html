LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Increases the amount of experience gained when completing a floor.
  • Increases the chance of getting the Gordie pet drop; increases again at 200 million experience
  • Increases the amount of tokens gained from Dungeoneering token boxes
1
  • Access floor 1 of Daemonheim, as well as frozen floors
  • Fight the Gluttonous behemoth, Astea Frostweb, and Icy Bones at the end of any frozen floors
    • Respectively, receive primal boots, celestial shoes & celestial catalytic wands, and sagittarian vambraces & primal knives as drops
  • Use up to one bound piece of equipment at a time
  • Bind one stack of 125 arrows or runes, or 125 spell charges in a magical blastbox
2
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim easy tasks
3
  • Access floor 2 of Daemonheim
4
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
5
  • Access floor 3 of Daemonheim
  • Fight the Luminescent Icefiend at the end of frozen floors 3-11
    • Receive primal daggers and off-hand primal daggers as a drop
6
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim easy tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim medium tasks
7
  • Access floor 4 of Daemonheim
8
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
9
  • Access floor 5 of Daemonheim
10
  • Access the Edgeville Dungeon Chaos Druid resource dungeon
11
  • Access floor 6 of Daemonheim
  • Fight Plane-freezer Lakhrahnaz at the end of frozen floors 6-11
    • Receive primal gauntlets as a drop
12
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
13
  • Access floor 7 of Daemonheim
14
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
15
  • Access floor 8 of Daemonheim
  • Access the Dwarven Mine resource dungeon
16
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
17
  • Access floor 9 of Daemonheim
  • Fight To'Kash the Bloodchiller at the end of frozen floors 9-11
    • Receive celestial gloves as a drop
18
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
19
  • Access floor 10 of Daemonheim
20
  • Access the Edgeville Dungeon Hill Giant resource dungeon
  • Use up to two bound pieces of equipment at a time
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim easy tasks
21
  • Access floor 11 of Daemonheim
  • Collect charms with the charming imp (with 22 Summoning)
22
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
23
  • Access floor 12 of Daemonheim, as well as abandoned floors
  • Fight the Skeletal Horde, Hobgoblin Geomancer, and Bulwark beast at the end of any abandoned floors
    • Respectively, receive primal full helms, sagittarian boots, and primal hatchets & primal pickaxes as drops
  • Required to complete the Daemonheim Easy Tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim easy tasks
24
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
25
  • Access floor 13 of Daemonheim
  • Store gems in a gem bag (with 25 Crafting)
  • Access the Karamja Volcano resource dungeon
  • Use the scroll of life (with 25 Farming)
26
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
27
  • Access floor 14 of Daemonheim
28
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
29
  • Access floor 15 of Daemonheim
  • Fight the Unholy cursebearer at the end of abandoned floors 15-17
    • Receive celestial catalytic staves as a drop
30
  • Wear arcane pulse necklaces (with 30 Magic)
  • Wield magical blastboxes (with 30 Magic and 30 Runecrafting)
  • Wear twisted bird skull necklaces (with 30 Prayer)
  • Access the Daemonheim Peninsula resource dungeon
31
  • Access floor 16 of Daemonheim
32
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
33
  • Access floor 17 of Daemonheim
34
  • Required for two of the Daemonheim medium tasks
35
  • Access floor 18 of Daemonheim, as well as furnished floors
  • Fight Rammernaut, Stomp, and Har'Lakk the Riftsplitter at the end of any furnished floors
    • Respectively, receive primal mauls, sagittarian coifs & sagittarian shields, and primal rapiers & off-hand primal rapiers as drops
  • Store coal in a coal bag (with 35 Mining)
  • Required to start Salt in the Wound
  • Access the Baxtorian Falls resource dungeon
  • The number of arrows or runes that can be bound increases by 100, to 225 (after Salt in the Wound)
  • Required to complete two of the Daemonheim Medium Tasks
36
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
37
  • Access floor 19 of Daemonheim
38
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
39
  • Access floor 20 of Daemonheim
  • Fight Lexicus Runewright at the end of furnished floors 20-29
    • Receive celestial hoods & celestial catalytic orbs as drops
40
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
41
  • Access floor 21 of Daemonheim
42
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
43
  • Access floor 22 of Daemonheim
44
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
45
  • Access floor 23 of Daemonheim
  • Fight Sagittare at the end of furnished floors 23-29
    • Receive sagittarian arrows and sagittarian longbows as drops
  • Wield gravite rapiers, gravite longswords, and gravite 2h swords (with 45 Attack)
  • Wield gravite shortbows (with 45 Ranged)
  • Wield gravite staves (with 45 Magic)
  • Wield law staves (with 40 Attack and 45 Magic)
  • Attach a longbow sight to a maple shieldbow to produce a maple longbow (sighted) (with 45 Ranged)
  • Access the Mining Guild resource dungeon (with 60 Mining)
  • Attach a longbow sight to a magic shieldbow to produce a magic longbow (sighted) (with 55 Ranged)
46
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
47
  • Access floor 24 of Daemonheim
48
  • Wield tomes of frost (with 48 Magic)
  • Wield amulets of zealots (with 48 Prayer)
49
  • Access floor 25 of Daemonheim
  • Use the scroll of cleansing (with 49 Herblore)
50
  • Use up to three bound pieces of equipment at a time
  • Wear arcane blast necklaces (with 50 Magic)
  • Required to start A Clockwork Syringe
  • Wear spirit capes (with 50 Defence and 50 Summoning)
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim hard tasks
51
  • Access floor 26 of Daemonheim
  • Fight Night-gazer Khighorahk at the end of furnished floors 26-29
    • Receive primal plateskirts as a drop
52
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
53
  • Access floor 27 of Daemonheim
  • Wield nature staves (with 40 Attack and 53 Magic)
54
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
55
  • Access floor 28 of Daemonheim
  • Use the scroll of efficiency (with 55 Smithing)
  • Access the Taverley hellhound resource dungeon
56
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
57
  • Access floor 29 of Daemonheim
58
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
59
  • Access floor 30 of Daemonheim, as well as abandoned 2 floors
  • Fight Shadow-forger Ihlakhizan at the end of any abandoned 2 floors
    • Receive primal kiteshields as a drop
60
  • Use the scroll of quick teleportation
  • Use the scroll of dexterity (with 60 crafting)
  • Use the anti-poison totem (with 60 Defence and 70 Herblore)
  • Use the anti-poison totem (pocket) (with 70 Herblore)
  • Use the split dragon tooth necklace (with 60 Prayer)
  • Use the scroll of proficiency (with 60 Construction)
  • Access the Taverley blue dragon resource dungeon
61
  • Access floor 31 of Daemonheim
62
  • Wear rings of vigour (with 62 Attack)
63
  • Access floor 32 of Daemonheim
64
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
65
  • Access floor 33 of Daemonheim
  • Fight Bal'lak the Pummeller at the end of abandoned 2 floors 33-35
    • Receive primal warhammers & off-hand primal warhammers as a drop
  • Use the scroll of renewal to unlock the Rapid Renewal prayer (with 65 Prayer)
  • Access the Varrock Sewers resource dungeon
  • Required to complete two of the Daemonheim hard tasks
66
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
67
  • Access floor 34 of Daemonheim
68
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
69
  • Access floor 35 of Daemonheim
70
  • Wear arcane stream necklaces (with 80 Magic)
  • Wield celestial surgeboxes (with 70 Magic and 70 Runecrafting)
  • Access the Chaos Tunnels resource dungeon
  • Collect Dungeoneering fragments
71
  • Access floor 36 of Daemonheim, as well as occult floors
  • Fight the Skeletal trio, Gravecreeper, and Runebound behemoth at the end of any occult floors
    • Respectively, receive sagittarian shortbows & sagittarian arrows, primal chainbodies, and celestial robe bottoms & celestial shields as drops
  • Required to complete two of the Daemonheim Hard Tasks
72
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
73
  • Access floor 37 of Daemonheim
  • Wear mercenary's gloves (with 73 Ranged)
74
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • Access the Al Kharid mining site resource dungeon
  • Access floor 38 of Daemonheim
  • Required to complete the Desert Hard Tasks
  • Required to start Plague's End
76
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
77
  • Access floor 39 of Daemonheim
  • Fight the Necrolord at the end of occult floors 39-47
    • Receive sagittarian chaps & off-hand primal knives as drops
78
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
79
  • Access floor 40 of Daemonheim
80
  • Wield chaotic rapiers and off-hand chaotic rapiers, chaotic longswords and off-hand chaotic longswords, chaotic claws and off-hand chaotic claws, and chaotic mauls (with 80 Attack)
  • Wield chaotic crossbows and off-hand chaotic crossbows (with 80 Ranged)
  • Wield chaotic staves (with 80 Magic)
  • Wield chaotic kiteshields, eagle-eye kiteshields, and farseer kiteshields (with 80 Defence)
  • Own a sneakerpeeper as a pet (with 80 Summoning)
  • Access the Brimhaven Dungeon resource dungeon
81
  • Access floor 41 of Daemonheim
82
  • Access the Polypore Dungeon resource dungeon

    • Receive primal platelegs as a drop

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • Obtain the Frosty pet (with level 99 Summoning)

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • One of the requirements for the Tirannwn elite tasks
  • One of the requirements for the Daemonheim elite tasks
  • Unlock The Exile in player-owned port
  • Use up to four bound pieces of equipment at a time

  • Required to complete Daemonheim Elite Tasks
  • Access the Gorajo resource dungeon
  • One of the requirements for the Tirannwn elite tasks
