E-I-E-DI-NO is an achievement that requires the player to complete all of the breeding logs at the Ranch Out of Time on Anachronia listed below.

Completing this achievement as well as E-I-E-I-O rewards the player with the title: Old Mc[Name].

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement, and all of the other achievements that are required to complete it, are references to the children's nursery rhyme Old MacDonald Had a Farm.
  • Between 25 November 2019 and 27 October 2020, this achievement had a grace period meaning that they were technically a requirement for the trimmed completionist cape, but did not count as such until the grace period ended after which the requirement was actually required.
