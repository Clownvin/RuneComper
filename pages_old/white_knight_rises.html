White Knight Rises is an achievement that requires the player to obtain the White Knight rank of Master by killing 1,300 black knights.

The best location to complete this task would be in Taverley Dungeon at the very south of the dungeon. Setting up a Dwarf multicannon while using AoE abilities makes completing this task very quick.

Alternatively, players may kill Elite Black Knights as they count as 12 normal black knight kills, and they are relatively fast to kill, but require more attention.

When reaching each rank, the player gets a message: Congratulations! You are now a White Knight [Rank]!
