Know Thy Measure is a mystery and achievement. This mystery is needed to access Moksha ritual site. It involves the poem found in Next of Kin being translated into dragonkin language.

Even though the player learns some mechanics used in the puzzles featured in this mystery during the Desperate Measures quest, the quest is not required for this mystery.

Walkthrough[edit | edit source]

Image Steps
  • Head to the outpost by following the map or using the dragonkin teleportation network. Enter through the north door. Optionally, bring 20 un-noted Ancient Vis to activate the teleportation device.
(Optional) Activating the teleport portal
  • Bring 20 un-noted Ancient vis with to power up the teleportation device inside, if the first Orthen teleportation device has been activated and the research Incomplete Portal Network I has been completed.
  • The dragonkin pylon is just inside the door. Click on it to interact until it displays digits.
  • Click each symbol to change it until they show: 3, 1, 5. (Hep)
    (Hover the mouse over the symbol to see the number. Clicking the symbol increments +1. When done successfully it will say "You hear a small beeping noise.")
  • Cycle symbols to: 4, 1, 4. (Kal)
    (When done successfully, it will say "You hear a small beeping noise.")
  • Cycle symbols to: 1, 6, 7. (Ort)
    (When done successfully, the symbols will disappear.)
  • Continue dialogue.
  • Inspect the strange device which appears in your inventory, and choose the option to peer into it (1).
  • Lodestone teleport back to Anachronia. Proceed to the entrance to Moksha ritual site. Optionally, bring 20 un-noted orthenglass to activate the teleportation device.
  • Interact with the statue to enter the pass phrase. Choose chat option 1 in first dialog list, 2 in second dialog list, 3 in the third, 4 in the fourth.
    (Sha, Cra, Lith, Kab)
  • Cycle the symbols to: 5, 1, 6. (Nos)
    (If done successfully, it will say "You hear a small beeping noise.")
  • Cycle the symbols to: 4, 1, 8. (Kay)
    (If done successfully, it will say "You hear a small beeping noise.")
  • Cycle the symbols to: 5, 1, 4. (Mok)
    (If done successfully, the symbols will disappear and a door will open.)
  • Go down the stairs to solve the mystery.
(Optional) Activating the teleport portal
  • Bring 20 un-noted Orthenglass with to power up the teleportation device inside, if the first two Orthen teleportation device have been activated and the research Incomplete Portal Network II has been completed.
