You Know You Want It! is an achievement that requires the player to go to the Fossegrimen and charm her into accepting a raw bass. Sacrifice a raw bass while having an uncharged enchanted lyre in the inventory and having the ring of charos (a) equipped, and talk to the Fossegrimen to have the lyre recharged. She is located south-west of Rellekka.

The lyre must not be fully charged (60 charges) for this to work, so be sure to use at least one charge before attempting this achievement.

This must be done at the altar; the remote contact option on the Fremennik sea boots 1 does not count.
