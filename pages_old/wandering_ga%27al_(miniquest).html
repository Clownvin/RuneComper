Wandering Ga'al (miniquest) is a miniquest that was released in January 2013 as a response on an ongoing hunt to translate the final bookcase in Brink of Extinction, after a Jagex Moderator mistakenly told the players there was a way to read it. The players searched for a little over a month before a solution was finally implemented.[1]

Content developer Mod Stu coded the miniquest in little over an hour, and continuously gave hints towards finding the Ga'al on the forums. The solution was eventually found on 14 May 2013, 6 months after the bookcase was added to the game.[2][3][4]

Meeting the Ga'al[edit | edit source]

The wandering Ga'al near the bookshelf following the completion of the miniquest.

TzHaar-Ga'al-Kot can be encountered any time after completing The Brink of Extinction, by using a ring of visibility on a ring of stone anywhere outside of the TzHaar City.

TzHaar-Ga'al-Kot will then appear, thanking you for finding him. Ask him if he's able to read the Ancient TzHaar language, and he will agree to meet you at the bookcase in the Fight Cauldron.

Miniquest complete!

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Hankmeister was the first player to complete the Wandering Ga'al miniquest.[5]
  • The jingle that plays when completing the miniquest is re-used from the 2016 Easter event.
  • Before the update on 24 October 2022, the miniquest had a number of specific conditions for the Ga'al to appear, which were originally meant to be figured out by the community based on Mod Stu's hints. After nearly a decade he removed them as a part of his Game Jam project, since they were deemed to unnecessarily convolute the requirements after they were discovered.[6] The original conditions that had to be met before using the rings were as follows:
    • Obtain all required obsidian shards before proceeding any further.
    • Equip at least one piece of obsidian armour and donate 15,000 Tokkul to the coffer in the Fight Cauldron. This will boost the armour reduction effect of obsidian armour for the next 20 minutes. The next steps must be done within those 20 minutes.
    • Unequip any obsidian armour, TokHaar-Kal, Igneous Kal and fire capes you have equipped. Having any of these items equipped prevents you from seeing him.
    • Equip the uncharged Tokkul-Zo. If you have a charged Tokkul-Zo, the charges can be removed by killing TzHaar creatures, or by destroying the ring and reclaiming it in its uncharged state from TzHaar-Mej-Jeh or TzHaar-Mej-Ak. After the miniquest, it will cost 50,000 tokkul to charge it again (as the uncharged ring cannot be used for teleports).
    • Leave TzHaar City with a number of obsidian shards equal to R÷10 rounded down, where R is the current Runedate, which may be found by reading the captain's log from Player Owned Ports.
      • For example: if the current Runedate is 4347.8, you will need 434 shards.
