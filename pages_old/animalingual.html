Animalingual is an achievement which requires the player to fully upgrade the cramulet by adding all six speaking amulets to it.

Jewellery Quest Abilities
Ghostspeak amulet The Restless Ghost (added during Do No Evil) Speaking to ghosts
Monkeyspeak amulet Monkey Madness (added during Do No Evil) Speaking to monkeys
Camulet Enakhra's Lament Speaking to camels and teleporting to Enakhra's Temple
Catspeak amulet[cram 1] Icthlarin's Little Helper Speaking to cats
Catspeak amulet (e)[cram 1] A Tail of Two Cats Speaking to cats and locating Bob
Crocspeak amulet Back to the Freezer Speaking to crocodiles
Cabbagespeak amulet Gower Quest Speaking to cabbages
  1. ^ a b The catspeak amulet and catspeak amulet (e) are mutually exclusive, though adding the enchanted version while the normal version is already in the cramulet will instead upgrade the amulet inside.
