On a Mission from...the Gods is an achievement that requires the player to complete all of the tasks given by the God Emissaries, and to claim the reward helmet.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • update 18 April 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name of the achievement is a reference to a quote from the 1980 film The Blues Brothers.
