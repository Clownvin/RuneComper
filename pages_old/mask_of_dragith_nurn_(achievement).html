Mask of Dragith Nurn is an achievement that requires the player to assemble the five parts of the Mask of Dragith Nurn from the Lumbridge Catacombs which rewards the cosmetic override of the mask as well as the mask as an item.

Mask parts[edit | edit source]

The mask consists of 5 parts dropped by the following creatures in the Lumbridge Catacombs.

Part Dropped by
Mask part 1 Level 1 warped cockroaches in room 1
Mask part 2 Level 4 corpse spiders in room 2; level 7 warped flies in room 3
Mask part 3 Level 9 crawling corpse torsos in room 4; level 12 warped rats in room 5; level 15 skeleton in room 6
Mask part 4 Level 16 warped bats in room 7; level 18 corpse archers in room 8; level 19 skoblins in room 9; level 21 corpse mages in room 10
Mask part 5 Level 26 Dragith Nurn in the final room (11)
