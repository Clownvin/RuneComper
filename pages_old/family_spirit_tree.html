Family Spirit Tree is an achievement that requires the player to plant the spirit tree in Brimhaven, Port Sarim, Etceteria, and Manor Farm before talking to Glouron to claim the Farming XP lamp. The fourth tree may only be planted after the first three trees have been health-checked and Glouron has been informed.

The spirit tree must be fully grown and checked before being able to claim the Farming XP lamp, and therefore the achievement.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The achievement name is a reference to family trees, charts showing familial relations in a tree structure.
  • This achievement only required level 89 Farming prior to the release of the Manor Farm spirit tree patch, and players who had earned it prior did not have it re-locked after the update.
