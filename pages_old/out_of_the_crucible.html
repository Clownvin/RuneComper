Overview[edit | edit source]

Alignment Bandosian
Dig site Warforge Dig Site
Requirements
  • 83 Archaeology [B]
Required items These items can be obtained before and/or during the mystery.

None

Rewards 16,000 experience

Steps[edit | edit source]

  • Study the huge barricade blocking further access into the Warforge Dig Site.
  • Excavate the barricade until it's completely removed, requiring level 83 Archaeology (boostable).
  • Enter the revealed tunnels entrance (either left or right) to complete the mystery.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The player le me was the first player who completed this mystery which was shown in-game with the announcement: News: le me is the first to solve the 'Out of the Crucible' mystery!
  • This mystery and the Into the Forge mystery are a reference to the idiom Out of the frying pan into the fire.
