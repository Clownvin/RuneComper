Gross Misconduct is an achievement that requires the player to kill 144 demons in the Wilderness.

Completing it is required for the completion of the Civil War I miniquest, which serves as a narrative wrapper for the achievement.

Hellhounds, lesser demons and greater demons in the Forinthry Dungeon or Wilderness Crater are relatively easy demons to kill for this achievement, due to their low life points and high number of spawns.

Other demons that count for this achievement include abyssal demons, abyssal beasts, abyssal lords, abyssal savages, black demons, greater demon ash lords, greater demon berserker, Kal'gerion demons, Ripper Demons, waterfiends and gargoyles. Chaos Elemental does not count towards this achievement.

You can track the amount of demons that you have killed in the Hero > Achievements > Lore > Combat.

Update history[edit | edit source]

  • patch 22 May 2023 (Update):
    • Demons now only count as 1 to 1 for progress.
  • update 17 October 2022 (Update):
    • Required to complete Civil War I
    • The tier 3 upgrade is no longer granted when completing this achievement. It's been moved to be a reward for completing Civil War I (miniquest). Players who previously completed this achievement kept the upgrade.
  • update 1 August 2022 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • A gross is a group of 144 (or a dozen dozen) items.
  • Originally the tier 3 upgrade of the Infernal Puzzle Box was awarded directly upon the completion of this achievement. However the reward was moved to its wrapper miniquest, Civil War I, in an update on 17 October 2022.
