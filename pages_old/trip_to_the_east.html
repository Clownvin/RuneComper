Trip to the East is an achievement that requires the player to complete one of the Trio storylines in the player-owned port, Quin, Hyu-Ji, Eastern Curiosities or Guardians of the World.

Steps to achieve[edit | edit source]

  1. Players must first complete all the story voyages from the adventurers:
    1. For Quin - Requires completion of the story voyages of The Whaler, The Assassin, and The Occultist
    2. For Hyu-Ji - Requires completion of the story voyages of The Convict, The Biologist, and The Missionary
    3. For Eastern Curiosities - Requires completion of the story voyages of The Chef, The Trapper, and The Architect
    4. For Guardians of the World - Requires completion of the story voyages of The Tengu, The Memory, and The Exile.
  2. After the story missions are complete, when 2 of the 3 related adventurers are at the port, a Trio story voyage will appear, as follows:
  3. Three Trio voyages with the same set of adventurers must be completed in order to award this achievement.

Trio missions[edit | edit source]

Any one of the following series of Trio missions must be completed to obtain this achievement.

To receive Trio story missions, all 3 adventurers in each trio must be in the port for their respective voyages to appear. The voyages are received in order as shown below (for each trio; you can complete more than one trio storyline at once). Once all 3 final story missions are completed, then you have completed the storyline for that trio. Completion of 1 storyline is a requirement for the completionist cape.

Guardians of the World[edit | edit source]

Requires completion of the story voyages of The Tengu, The Memory, and The Exile.

This storyline requires having the Shield region unlocked and a total distance travelled of 6,000,000 - the maximum distance that can be travelled in player-owned ports.
