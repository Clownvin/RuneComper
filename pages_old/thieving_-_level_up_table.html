LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level increases the chances of success of pickpocketing
  • Increases the amount of experience for looting Coshing volunteers.
  • Increases the chance of getting the Ralph pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
