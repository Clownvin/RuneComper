I Can See For Miles and Miles is an achievement that requires the player to unlock all beacon keepers and fully unlock the beacon map by providing the keepers with macaw pouches.

Items required[edit | edit source]

  • 12 macaw pouches

Items required for unlocking all the beacons and beacon keepers:

  • White tree fruit for Ube
  • Davy kebbit hat for Stubthumb
  • Catspeak amulet and 2 iron bars for Doronbol
  • 2 planks, 4 nails, and a raw or cooked lava eel, for Crate (the lava eel is required even if Crate has been given one previously)
  • 3 jute fibres and a needle for the level 87 beacon
  • 2 iron bars OR 2 planks (cannot be protean) and 4 nails, for the level 89 beacon

Basic guide[edit | edit source]

Use the macaw pouches on the beacon keepers to give them their macaws. Alternatively, the chat option "Is it possible to get information about beacons that are further away?" will also work.

  • Beacon level 43 (Beacon keeper: Blaze Sharpeye): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 43 (Beacon keeper: Squire Fyre): simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 48 (Beacon keeper: Orry; Next to the Fort Forinthry lodestone): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 53 (Beacon keeper: Ube; Wilderness level: 1): Give Ube the white tree fruit then the macaw pouch. Nearby giant rats will aggro on you regardless of level at this beacon which will interrupt dialogue.
  • Beacon level 59 (Beacon keeper: Waldo; Wilderness level: 2): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 62 (Beacon keeper: Gjalp): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps. Requires completion of all four levels of the Stronghold of Security.
  • Beacon level 68 (Beacon keeper: Brother Fintan): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 72 (Beacon keeper: Stubthumb): Give Stubthumb the davy kebbit hat then the macaw pouch.
  • Beacon level 76 (Beacon keeper: Doronbol): A Tail of Two Cats must be completed to give Macaw pouch for keeper information.
  • Beacon level 79 (Beacon keeper: Crate; requires running through Wilderness to level 13): Give Crate a raw or cooked lava eel then the macaw pouch.
  • Beacon level 83 (Beacon keeper: Egg): Simply give the pouch with no extra steps.
  • Beacon level 87 - (Beacon keeper: Nanuq): Give Nanuq the pouch and repair the windbreak with level 60 Crafting, a needle and 3 jute fibres.
  • Beacon level 89: No beacon keeper.
  • Beacon level 92: No beacon keeper.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name of this achievement is a reference to the song "I Can See for Miles" by British rock band The Who.
