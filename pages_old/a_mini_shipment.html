A Mini Shipment is an achievement that requires the player to loot the trunk in the azure room of the Temple of Aminishi using a trunk key.

This achievement can be completed in story mode as well as in normal mode.

Steps[edit | edit source]

This requires starting the quest Curse of the Black Stone until the part with the the Jade Spider on Goshima. Getting to this stage of the quest requires easy combat and no items.

  1. Go to Goshima (if Curse of the Black Stone is incomplete, select No to searching for the Jade Spider), enter the gate, and search the eastern trunk outside the north-easternmost house to find the azure key.
  2. Go to the Temple of Aminishi on Aminishi. The azure door is in the first room on the northern wall on the north-east side. Unlock it with the key.
  3. Enter the room and walk through it and then leave through the other door to access north-eastern Aminishi.
  4. Dig the mound of sand on the northern part of the island to receive the trunk key.
  5. Return to the azure room and use the key on the trunk to complete the achievement. This will cause the following message to be shown: "You plunder the trunk and find many useful supplies!"

Rewards[edit | edit source]

The trunk contains the following:

  • Superior scrimshaw of sacrifice
  • Superior scrimshaw of vampyrism
  • Ports chi pack
  • Ports bone pack
  • Ports plate pack

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Initially this achievement was only part of the Elite Dungeons subcategory of the Combat category. A week after the release (4 March 2019) this achievement also became part of the Master Quest Cape subcategory of the Completionist category.
  • The name of the achievement is a play on Aminishi, the name of the island that the trunk is found on.[1]
