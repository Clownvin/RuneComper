Potting Is Such Sweet Sorrow is an achievement that requires the player to help Bill—who is located near the mystical tree on the west coast of Anachronia—realise his potential by completing the below steps. Bill will not have any chat options about the magic pot until the achievement Mycomanagement is completed, which involves helping Bill with Eggsperimentation, requiring 95 Firemaking.

Steps
Agree to get Bill's magic pot back from Ben
Ask Ben about his brother's pot
Give Ben an empty plant pot
Safely return Bill's magic pot

Instead of an empty plant pot a full one can also be used.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement, and the name of the zygomites, is in reference to the British Television series the Flower Pot Men, also known as Bill and Ben, The Flower Pot Men from 1952 that was remade in 2001.
  • The name of the achievement Potting Is Such Sweet Sorrow, is in reference to Shakespeare in the play Romeo and Juliet, where Juliet says "Yet I should kill thee with much cherishing. Good night, good night! Parting is such sweet sorrow. That I shall say good night till it be morrow."[1]
