Abyssal Shortcut is an achievement that requires the player to unlock the asylum gate in the Senntisten Asylum, and open the shortcut to the lower level of the asylum.

The gate is located near Abyssal savages, in the upper level of the asylum. It can be unlocked using a gate key that can be obtained as a drop from the Abyssal lords. After the gate is unlocked, attaching a rope to the broken railing is required to complete the shortcut.
