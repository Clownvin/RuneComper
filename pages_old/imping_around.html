Imping Around is an achievement that requires the player to catch one of each type of impling for Daffyd after speaking to him.

Requirements[edit | edit source]

Impling
Baby impling Magpie impling
Young impling Ninja impling
Gourmet impling Pirate impling
Earth impling Divine impling
Essence impling Dragon impling
Eclectic impling Zombie impling
Spirit impling Kingly impling
Nature impling Crystal impling

Strategy[edit | edit source]

An easy way to achieve this is by visiting the following locations, and world-hopping in order to discover multiple types of Implings at that location:

  • Gorajo resource dungeon in Prifddinas
    • The Dungeoneering skillcape teleport can be used to quickly enter this location ("Gorajo hoardstalker dungeon")
    • Impling spawns are very frequent, and up to 3 unique types of implings can be found on a single world.
    • High level implings are more frequent at this location. However, Dragon and Kingly implings do not spawn at this location.
  • Puro Puro
    • All types of implings spawn at this location, including Dragon and Kingly implings, with exception of Crystal implings which can only spawn in Prifddinas.
  • Al Kharid resource dungeon
    • The Dungeoneering skillcape teleport can be used to quickly enter this location ("Al Kharid hidden mine")
    • Impling spawns are very frequent, and up to 5 unique types of implings can be found on a single world.
    • Higher level implings are uncommon at this location. Dragon and Kingly impling spawns are very rare.
  • Menaphos
    • Fishing and woodcutting in Menaphos has a chance to spawn implings. This requires tier 4 Menaphos reputation.

It is worth noting that implings received from opening the Motherlode Maw, or exchanging 10 crystal motherlode shards with Wythien do count towards the achievement. All types of impling, even Crystal and Kingly implings, may be obtained this way.
