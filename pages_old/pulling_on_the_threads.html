Pulling on the Threads is an achievement that requires the player to discover all of the memory fragments in each chapter in The Needle Skips and then claim the reward from Megan.

The player can discover the chapters by typing in keywords related to the quest and its dialogue. A list of possible words can be found on the quest page, and requires the Shard of the Needle to be submitted. Finding each keyword for each memory fragment is not necessary.

You must leave the instanced quest area before speaking with Megan to complete the achievement.
