LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • With each Firemaking level, it becomes slightly easier to light a set of logs
  • Increases the chance of getting the Bernie pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each Firemaking level slightly increases the experience earned from Clan Citadel kilns
  • Reduces the chance of the hand cannon exploding

  • Burn normal evil trees

  • One of the requirements for Forgiveness of a Chaos Dwarf

  • Light arctic pine logs with a bow (after learning how in Barbarian Training)

  • Light eucalyptus pyre logs

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • Bonfires will boost maximum life points by 8%

  • Create and burn Dwarf weed incense sticks

  • One of the requirements for The Void Stares Back

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • One of the requirements for The Firemaker's Curse

  • Burn magic logs, and add them to bonfires

  • Create Infernal magic incense sticks

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • Light the beacon east of Death Plateau (after All Fired Up and Heroes' Quest, and with 42 Construction)

  • Light yew logs with a bow (after learning how in Barbarian Training)

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • One of the requirements for Pieces of Hate

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members

  • Light the beacon near the God Wars Dungeon entrance (after All Fired Up and Troll Stronghold, and with 81 Summoning and 60 Agility or 60 Strength)

  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
89
  • Light the beacon near the Chaos Temple in level 38 Wilderness (after All Fired Up and with 70 Smithing or 59 Construction)
90
  • Bonfires will boost maximum life points by 10%
  • Burn elder evil trees

  • Light elder logs, and add them to bonfires
  • One of the requirements for the Tirannwn elite tasks

  • Access Char's training cave and own fire creatures as pets (after The Firemaker's Curse)

  • Create and burn Fellstalk incense sticks

  • Light the beacon on the Frozen Waste Plateau in deep Wilderness (after All Fired Up)
  • Wield the inferno adze (after All Fired Up)
  • Burn Driftwood found on Uncharted Isles
  • One of the requirements for the Varrock elite tasks
93
  • Nourish nourished ancient effigies in Firemaking
94
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
95
  • Burn three different types of incenses at a time
  • Light magic logs with a bow (after learning how in Barbarian Training)
  • Nourish sated ancient effigies in Firemaking
  • Light Elder pyre logs
96
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
97
  • Nourish gorged ancient effigies in Firemaking
98
  • No new abilities unlocked for free players or members
99
  • Skill mastery
  • Ability to purchase and equip the Firemaking cape and hood from Ignatius Vulcan at Seers' Village
  • Light barbarian pyre ships made from elder logs (after learning how in Barbarian Training and with 99 Crafting)
  • Light Elder logs with a bow (after learning how in Barbarian Training)

  • True skill mastery
  • Ability to purchase and equip the Firemaking master cape from Ignatius Vulcan at Seers' Village
