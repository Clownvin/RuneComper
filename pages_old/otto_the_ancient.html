Otto the Ancient is an achievement that requires the player to find all 26 ancient pages from rummaging skeletons and fighting monsters within the Ancient Cavern. See here for a list of pages.

Due to the time it takes to kill them and their low drop rate of pages, it is not advised to kill mithril dragons unless also hunting a Dragon full helm drop. One hour of killing Mithril dragons nets 2-3 pages on average, whereas searching skeletons can yield 10 or more.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

For both strategies listed below, it is recommended to summon a Lorehound with the Lore Helper perk unlocked. This will increase the chance of receiving ancient pages. Bladed Dive, Double Surge, and Barge will also be extremely useful for getting around the cavern faster, especially when used alongside the mobile perk or the Shadow's Grace relic. Finally, as rummaging skeletons can inflict typeless damage onto the player, it is advised to bring food or an Enhanced Excalibur. The Excalibur's cooldown will be reset shortly after world hopping, so it can be used repeatedly.

Combat-intensive strategy[edit | edit source]

This strategy involves killing many monsters, as well as rummaging the skeletons. Dual-wield melee is recommended, so the player can take advantage of Barge and Bladed Dive (if unlocked). Players with cinderbane gloves should wear them to deal extra damage in the form of poison.

Start at the southwest corner (below the stairs of Kuradel's location). Kill the nearby barbarian spirits and skeleton, then run east and rummage the skeleton. Run south to kill the barbarian spirit, then east to kill one more. Skip the lost barbarian, who will not drop any pages. Run northeast and kill the skeleton, then rummage the skeleton. Run west then north, skipping the confused barbarian, who will not drop any pages. Run to the northeast, near the waterfiends. It is possible to barge onto the skeleton warlord from around the corner, saving a bit of time. Rummage the final skeleton, then hop worlds and go through the sequence in reverse order.

When rummaging each of the skeletons, it is likely that a skeleton will spawn. It is advised to kill it, as it has a chance to drop pages.

Pacifist strategy[edit | edit source]

Players who are low level or do not wish to engage in combat can simply focus on rummaging each of the three skeleton spawns in the cavern. If a skeleton spawns, ignore it and run away. After rummaging all three skeletons, hop worlds and repeat.
