LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Mining slightly increases a player's chance of successfully mining ore
  • Increases the chance of getting the Rocky pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level in Mining slightly increases the experience earned from a Clan Citadel mine

  • Mine rune essence from the Rune Essence mine (after Rune Mysteries)
  • Mine with bronze pickaxes and the dwarven army axe
