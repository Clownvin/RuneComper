Relight My Fire is an achievement that requires the player to fully upgrade the campfire in War's Retreat. This requires 6,000 Marks of War, and 1,000 each of oak logs, willow logs, maple logs, yew logs, magic logs, and elder logs. Each successive upgrade requires the previous one to be unlocked.

Unlock/item Marks of War level Description Campfire duration Cost in logs
Upgrade: Oak campfire 1,000 15 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used oak logs on it. 12 minutes 790,000
Upgrade: Willow campfire 1,000 30 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used willow logs on it. 18 minutes 731,000
Upgrade: Maple campfire 1,000 45 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used maple logs on it. 36 minutes 194,000
Upgrade: Yew campfire 1,000 60 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used yew logs on it. 54 minutes 325,000
Upgrade: Magic campfire 1,000 75 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used magic logs on it. 60 minutes 540,000
Upgrade: Elder campfire 1,000 90 Upgrades the campfire to give you the health duration as if you've used elder logs on it. 66 minutes 5,868,000
Total 6,000 8,448,000
