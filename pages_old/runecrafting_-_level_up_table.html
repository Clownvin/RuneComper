LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level gained in Runecrafting slightly increases the chance that players will craft multiple runes from a single essence. The chance to craft multiple runes becomes 100% when a certain level is reached. For example, players with 40 Runecrafting have a chance to craft 5 air runes from one essence, but players with 44 Runecrafting will always craft at least 5 air runes per essence
  • The success rate of siphoning runes in the Runespan increases with each level
  • The speed of attracting/repelling orbs in The Great Orb Project increases as well as your chances of successfully removing barriers
  • Increases the chance of getting the Rue pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level increases your chances of crafting High level runes at the Ourania Altar, therefore increasing your experience and usually, increasing your profit
  • Each level increases the total amount of pure essence stored in the rune chest.
  • Each level increases the chance of obtaining magical thread when crafting runes via the Abyss.
