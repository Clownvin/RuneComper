Eye for an Eye (miniquest) is an achievement that requires the player to complete the miniquest with the same name.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be included—see here for how to help out!
  • patch 5 December 2022 (Update):
    • Became a requirement for the I've Got a Little List (of Miniquests) meta achievement, previously being directly required by Completionist Cape and Master Quest Cape.
  • update 18 April 2017 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Prior to the update on 8 April 2019, this achievement was called Arc V - Eye for an Eye and its description was "Complete the 'Eye for an Eye' miniquest."
