E-I-E-I-O is an achievement that requires the player to complete all of the breeding log at the player-owned farm by completing the achievements listed below.

Completing this achievement as well as E-I-E-DI-NO rewards the player with the title: Old Mc[Name].

  • update 24 June 2019 (Update):
    • Moved the achievement from the Farming subcategory of the Skills achievements to the Trimmed Completionist Cape subcategory of Completionist achievements.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement, and all of the other achievements that are required to complete it, are references to the children's nursery rhyme Old MacDonald Had a Farm.
  • Between 24 June and 24 July 2019, this achievement had a grace period meaning that they were technically a requirement for the trimmed completionist cape, but did not count as such until the grace period ended after which the requirement was actually required.
