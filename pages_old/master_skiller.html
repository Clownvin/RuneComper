Master Skiller is an achievement that tracks the player's progress towards skill levels needed for the completionist cape.

Currently the achievement requires level 99 in all skills, as well as level 120 in the following skills: Dungeoneering, Invention, Slayer, Farming, Herblore, and Archaeology.
