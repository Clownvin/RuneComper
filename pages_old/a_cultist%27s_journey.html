A Cultist's Journey is an achievement which requires the player to help The Curator, who is worried about a number of cults devoted to the Elder Gods, specifically Humans Against Monsters. Although there are no requirements, it is recommended to complete City of Senntisten beforehand as the tasks deal with direct consequences of events of that quest.

It was initially released as a series of narrative tasks as a part of Path of the Creators Yak Track on 23 August 2021. The tasks remained in game permanently after the event has ended. According to the in-game Quest List, this lore activity is not a miniquest as it is not listed there. On 16 January 2023 it was changed into an achievement.

Walkthrough[edit | edit source]

In order to start the activity, speak to the Curator, located north of the Burthorpe lodestone. He is concerned with reports of cults devoted to the Elder Gods appearing across Gielinor. His primary concern is Humans Against Monsters group and its leader, Johanhus Ulsbrecht. The Curator asks you to find and talk to Johanhus, and subsequently report back to Burthorpe. Johanhus's location will change every time you report back to the Curator.

You will need to talk to the Curator to report Jonahus's location and then once more before he spawns at the new location.

The locations are, in order:

  1. Next to the research notes in the Exam Centre near the Archaeology Guild. The quickest way to get there is to use the Dig Site pendant to teleport directly to the Centre. Alternatively the Archaeology journal can be used to teleport just north-west of the location.
  2. North of the Hunting expert in the Feldip Hills. This location can be accessed by running north from Oo'glog lodestone, west from AKS fairy ring or south from Lemantolly Undri gnome glider (shortcut 6, requires partial completion of One Small Favour).
  3. Inside the Senntisten Cathedral. Pontifex shadow ring, requiring the completion of City of Senntisten, teleports right next to Johanhus. The dig sites map in the Archaeology Guild, archaeology teleport or the master archaeologist's outfit can be used to teleport to the Senntisten Dig Site camp just north-east of the cathedral, provided the player has level 60 Archaeology. Alternatively Senntisten can be accessed through ancient door to the north of Archaeology Guild and right next to Lemanto Andra (shortcut 3) gnome glider.
  4. At the Senntisten, in Nodon Front, east of K'ril Tsutsaroth and near the entrance to the lower combat area. The quickest way to get there is to use a War's Retreat's or Max Guild's boss portal attuned to Kerapac, the bound and running south-east. Alternatively Johanhus can be reached by running west from the Cathedral, then north, then down the stairs to the east and slightly north. The Nodon dragonkin in the area are aggressive unless the Shard of Erebus is equipped.

Return to the Curator and speak to him one last time to finish the achievement.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The tasks were originally a part of Path of the Creators and accessing each one required a certain progress in the event. They were tasks 2, 15, 30, and 40 respectively. After the event was removed from the game, the tasks remained permanently, as they are considered part of the Elder God Wars quest series.
