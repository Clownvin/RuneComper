Purple Cat (miniquest) is a miniquest which is unlocked after the completion of the Swept Away quest, and was released during the 2008 Halloween event.

If you bring a hellcat of any kind to be dyed, it will be reverted to a normal cat.

Any form of cat will work for this quest and be able to be turned purple; including pet kitten.

Starting off[edit | edit source]

The map of Betty's basement.
  • Start by talking to Wendy, just west of the Draynor Village lodestone.
  • Ask Wendy about purple cats. She will tell you to bring her a cat and some magic unguent. She says you can get it from Lottie, Wendy's best friend, who is working with Betty in Port Sarim. Lottie is in Betty's Magic Emporium basement.
  • Talk to Lottie. She will tell you the unguent is in the same chest from the Swept Away quest, but Betty has strengthened security (last time you did it, she became paranoid) and you must move all 6 animals at least once this time.
  • Lottie will give you the magic slate again to view your progress during the miniquest.

The puzzle (take two)[edit | edit source]

  • This is essentially a smaller puzzle box, see the Treasure Trail Puzzle box guide, and can be solved using the same methods. By solving one of the vertical columns the rest is no different than the first puzzle.

Finishing up[edit | edit source]

  • Once the puzzle has been solved, open the chest and then search it, to obtain a vial of magic unguent.
  • Take this back to Wendy, along with a cat, to dye your cat purple. If you don't want to turn a cat purple, just bring the unguent without the cat. Wendy will take the unguent, and the miniquest will be completed.

Congratulations, miniquest complete!

Reward[edit | edit source]

  • Magic unguent
  • The ability to turn a cat's colour purple by talking to Wendy

Achievements[edit | edit source]

  • Purple Cat (miniquest) ( 10) – Complete this miniquest.
  • I've Got a Little List (of Miniquests) ( 0) – Complete all miniquests listed in the Quests interface. Does not include sagas.
