Stone Cold Observers is an achievement that requires the player to observe all five large dragonkin statues around Anachronia, excluding the statue at the Anachronia base camp.

  • Statue 1 (south-west) - West of Cricket the ancient zygomite, in the south-west part of the island.
  • Statue 2 (volcano) - North-west of the northern start of the Anachronia Agility Course, which is down the stairs to the north-west of the base camp.
  • Statue 3 (south-east) - Just south of Laniakea in the ruins on the south-eastern peninsula.
  • Statue 4 (north-east) - Just south-west of Leonard the ancient zygomite, south-east of the Anachronia canyon mine.
  • Statue 5 (east) - Just south of Penny the ancient zygomite, in the eastern-most ruins.
