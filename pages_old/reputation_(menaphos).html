Menaphos reputation status showing Tier 1 in Menaphos Overall, Imperial district, Port district, Merchant district, and Worker district.

Reputation is a mechanic that is used within Menaphos. By gaining reputation with the various faction leaders, rewards can be earned throughout the city.

Having the Unexpected Diplomacy relic power active at the mysterious monolith at the Archaeology Guild increases every reputation gain by 10%.

After completing Menaphos-related quests, all reputation gain is increased.

Quests Reputation increase
The Jack of Spades x1.25
Crocodile Tears x1.50
Our Man in the North x1.75
'Phite Club x2.00

Getting the maximum reputation in all districts is one of the requirements for the title, Sandy [Name].

Reputation tier descriptions[edit | edit source]

In the Reputation interface the player's reputation is listed with different descriptions for each faction depending on their reputation for the faction. This interface can be accessed by clicking any of the icons in the minigame HUD displayed when in Menaphos.

The minigame HUD uses the term "tier" when mouse cursor is over any of the five different sections, while the faction-specific tabs in the Reputation interface use the term "rank".

Tier or rank Description
1 Acquainted
2 Known
3 Familiar
4 Appreciated
5 Liked
6 Trusted
7 Adored
8 Loved
9 Revered
10 Worshipped

The city reputation shown in the overview tab of the Reputation interface doesn't involve descriptions.

Earning reputation[edit | edit source]

During the The Jack of Spades quest, 1,000 reputation is awarded for each of the four districts, as a reward for returning the stolen items to the district leaders. After completion of The Jack of Spades, all skilling activities within the walls of Menaphos earn reputation. In addition, skilling using any of the methods released along with Menaphos rewards a higher amount of reputation. Those activities which award reputation at an increased rate are chopping acadia trees, mining mineral deposits, fishing for desert sole, catfish, and beltfish, thieving in the Menaphos market, catching plover birds in Sophanem, and fighting corrupted and devourer creatures in the Sophanem Slayer Dungeon. Furthermore, the highest rates of reputation are earned by playing the Shifting Tombs minigame. Training divination with luminous energy in Sophanem does not award bonus reputation.

Players must first choose to which faction they want to align themselves before reputation can be earned. Right-click on a faction icon within the faction windows to choose a faction. If no faction is selected, reputation is not gained and the following message is seen instead: You have not currently selected a faction, so earned no reputation!

One-time rewards[edit | edit source]

  • Completing the Cats of Menaphos activity rewards the player with the purrn of previous postcat, which provides a one-time reward of 5,000 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader.
  • Completing the Jewels of the Elid activity rewards the player with the precious rhinestone cow, which provides a one-time reward of 5,000 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader.
  • Completing the Insects of the Desert activity rewards the player with a diamond honeycomb, which provides a one-time reward of 5,000 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader.
  • During The Jack of Spades quest players are awarded 1,000 reputation with each faction.
  • Completing the Crocodile Tears quest rewards the player with the relic of Crondis, which provides a one-time reward of 5,500 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader.
  • Completing the Our Man in the North quest rewards the player with the relic of Het, which provides a one-time reward of 5,500 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader.
  • Completing the 'Phite Club quest rewards the player with the relic of Tumeken, which provides a one-time reward of 7,500 reputation for a specific faction when handed in to a leader. The reputation the relic of Tumeken gives will however be boosted to 15,000 since the player must have completed 'Phite club in order to obtain it.
  • Interacting with Zahra to construct the cactus patch in the Imperial District provides a one-time reward of 750 reputation.
  • Each new monster added to the Slayer Codex rewards the player with 250 reputation.

Repeatable events[edit | edit source]

  • Interacting with soul obelisks in Menaphos can reward players up to 20,060 base reputation per day in a very short duration.
  • Interacting with Corrupted Scarabs in Menaphos can reward players reputation.
  • Completing a City Quest grants 2,000 base reputation, as well as a random-sized Menaphite gift offering.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Reputation is not spent and will not be lost. Instead, rewards are automatically unlocked upon reaching a reputation threshold. There are five different groups of reputation rewards, one for each district in Menaphos, and one for the overall city.

Overall[edit | edit source]

Tier Reputation Difference Reward Description
1 4,000 4,000 Grand Exchange Unlock the Menaphos Grand Exchange and bank.
2 6,600 2,600 Tumeken Standard Unlock the Tumeken Standard cosmetic override. The cooldown between city quests will be reduced slightly.
3 19,800 13,200 Crocodile Tears Gain access to the Crocodile Tears quest.
4 39,600 19,800 Surface Skilling Buff Get a chance to discover an Impling or Guthix butterfly while surface skilling around Menaphos. The cooldown between city quests will be reduced further.
5 66,000 26,400 Shifting Tombs Feather & Gold Buff Increase the amount of gold and feathers earned from Shifting Tombs. Also unlock the second tier of the Tumeken Standard cosmetic.
6 99,000 33,000 Our Man in the North Gain access to the Our Man in the North quest. The cooldown between city quests will be reduced significantly.
7 141,900 42,900 Slayer Feather Buff Get a 2% chance to save a Feather of Ma'at when fighting monsters in the Sophanem Slayer Dungeon. Also gain access to the VIP skilling area.
8 194,700 52,800 Blessed Tumeken Standard Unlock the final tier of the Tumeken Standard cosmetic override. Also start receiving Desert Pantheon aura fragments when finishing Shifting Tombs with all objectives completed. There will no longer be a cooldown between city quests.
9 254,100 59,400 'Phite Club Gain access to the 'Phite Club quest.
10 330,000 75,900 Magic Carpet Skilling Buff Receive extra experience in the form of magic carpet dust clouds during any skilling, even outside of Menaphos.

Reaching tier 10 overall reputation also unlocks the of Menaphos title.

Update history[edit | edit source]

This information has been compiled as part of the update history project. Some updates may not be includedsee here for how to help out!
  • patch 28 September 2020 (Update):
    • Safe-cracking in Menaphos now correctly rewards the player with Reputation.
  • ninja 11 June 2018 (Update):
    • Handing in a Relic in menaphos will now show you the correct amount of reputation you are going to gain.
  • patch 4 June 2018 (Update):
    • Menaphos reputation can now be gained at an increased rate based on completion of various quests:
      • Jack of Spades: 1.25x
      • Crocodile Tears: 1.5x
      • Our Man in the North: 1.75x
      • 'Phite Club: 2x
  • patch 4 September 2017 (Update):
    • An issue that was causing reputation achievement messages in Menaphos to spam players has been fixed.
  • patch 10 July 2017 (Update):
    • Progress bars in the Menaphos reputation interface now show the percentage of the progress within the current tier, rather than showing progress from 0 reputation.
    • Reaching the next tier of reputation in a Menaphos faction will now unlock any previous rewards that were missed for that faction due to reputation refunds or retroactive additions.
    • Performing any Menaphos surface skilling action when already tier 10 in a faction will also unlock any missed rewards for that faction.
  • patch 3 July 2017 (Update):
    • Menaphos reputation now caps correctly on the tooltip.
  • patch 26 June 2017 (Update):
    • Players will now receive 250 reputation for each new slayer monster added to the Slayer Codex, and retroactively awarded 250 reputation for each already added.
  • patch 12 June 2017 (Update):
    • The reputation gained whilst on and off task in the Sophanem slayer dungeon has been increased.
    • The reputation received when completing a city quest has been increased.
      • Retroactively, for any city quests you have completed prior to this update you will be given the reputation reward for them when logging in.
