LevelNew abilities
Each level
  • Each level in Cooking slightly increases the success rate of cooking food
  • Increases the chance of getting the Ramsay pet drop; continues to increase for virtual levels past 99, and again at 200 million experience
  • Each level in Cooking slightly increases the experience earned from a Clan Citadel Cooking plot
  • Each level increases the number of cheese that can be obtained from slicing a cheese wheel
