Ardougne Set Tasks - Medium
Release date 18 April 2017 (Update)
Members Yes
Description Given by the town crier in East Ardougne's market for completing all Medium Tasks in Ardougne.
RuneScore 10
Category Area Tasks
Rewards
  • Ardougne cloak 2
  • Magic lamp worth 7,500xp in a skill of your choice.
  • Wizard Cromperty will offer you 100 noted essence per day.
  • The cloak gains the following extra benefits:
    • Teleport to the Farming patch north of Ardougne once per day.
    • Improved rate of success when picking pockets in Ardougne.
    • Double length PvP immunity after pulling the Wilderness teleport lever.
    • 60% of items dropped in the Tower of Life will be noted.
    • Improved yield from crafting runes at Ourania Altar.
  • Talk to the town crier to toggle your ring of life teleport destination to Ardougne.
    • 50% chance of two chompy birds appearing when chompy hunting.
Requirements
  • See requirements

Ardougne Set Tasks - Medium is an achievement that is rewarded upon finishing the Medium Ardougne achievements.
