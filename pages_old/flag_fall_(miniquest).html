Flag Fall (miniquest) is a part of the Tales of the Arc miniquest series.

Details[edit | edit source]

To begin, speak to Boni at the Waiko market and ask about claiming an island, she will ask the player to help fix her loom. She requires five driftwood in order to do so. Driftwood can be found randomly on uncharted isles that require supplies for navigating to.

Supplies can be bought from Rosie on Waiko for 25 chimes per supply. 5 supplies (which cost 125 chimes or can be acquired for free once per day from the crate next to Rosie (supplies)) are required for a short junket, which is the shortest type of voyage into the uncharted isles. To access the uncharted isles, select Travel from Quartermaster Gully. Then, select the uncharted isles and the size of the voyage. Driftwood is not guaranteed on every island you visit, and the amount can vary. Isles visited for this miniquest cannot be saved (as you first have to finish the miniquest to unlock the ability to do so), so it is unwise to spend many chimes on a long voyage or medium expedition.

Once the five driftwood are obtained, return to Boni to finish the miniquest.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

  • 500 chimes
  • 2 Taijitu
  • Ability to claim an uncharted isle
  • Claim island flag in Waiko reward shop

Achievements[edit | edit source]

  • Flag Fall (miniquest) ( 10) – Complete this miniquest.
  • I've Got a Little List (of Miniquests) ( 0) – Complete all miniquests listed in the Quests interface. Does not include sagas.

Required for completing[edit | edit source]

Flag Fall (miniquest) is directly required for the following quests/miniquests:

  • Jed Hunter (miniquest)
It is therefore an indirect requirement for the following quests and miniquests:
  • Damage Control (miniquest)
  • Eye for an Eye (miniquest)
  • Final Destination (miniquest)
  • Ghosts from the Past (miniquest)
  • Harbinger (miniquest)
  • Tuai Leit's Own (miniquest)
