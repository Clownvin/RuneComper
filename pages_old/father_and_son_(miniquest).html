Getting started[edit | edit source]

Start the quest by talking to Irwinsson and ask to find out more about him. He will tell you his tale then ask you to speak to his father Sigli the Huntsman, who is located at the main gate of Rellekka. Head to Rellekka and talk to Sigli about Irwinsson. He will give you the greatbow he made for Irwinsson. Head back to Irwinsson and give him the bow.

Miniquest complete!

Rewards[edit | edit source]

  • Medium Hunter XP lamp

Achievements[edit | edit source]

  • Father and Son (miniquest) ( 10) – Complete this miniquest.
  • I've Got a Little List (of Miniquests) ( 0) – Complete all miniquests listed in the Quests interface. Does not include sagas.

Required for completing[edit | edit source]

Father and Son (miniquest) is not currently required for any quests or miniquests.
