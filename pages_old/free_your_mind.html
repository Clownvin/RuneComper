Overview[edit | edit source]

Alignment Dragonkin
Dig site Orthen Dig Site
Requirements
  • 106 Archaeology [B]
Required items These items can be obtained before and/or during the mystery.
  • Ethical Concerns
  • Higher States
  • Moksha
  • Raksha
Rewards 51,500 experience

Steps[edit | edit source]

  • Obtain Ethical Concerns, Higher States, Moksha, and Raksha pages from excavating Aughra remains (level 106 required) or Moksha device (level 108 required).
  • Talk to Mr Mordaut at the Anachronia Base Camp to solve the mystery.
