Mastery - Everlight is an achievement that requires the player to complete all of the listed achievements at the Everlight dig site.

This achievement had a grace period which ended on 30 September 2020.

Requirements[edit | edit source]

Achievement
Mysteries - Everlight ( 0) – Complete all of the listed achievements.
Research - Everlight ( 0) – Complete all of the listed research.
No Secrets Left to Steal ( 10) – Restore and claim from the statues of four famous icyene.
The Seeker's Job ( 20) – Catch the golden butterfly.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The player L33 was the first player to complete this achievement, on 5 April 2020, which was shown in-game with the announcement: News: L33 is the first to master the Everlight Dig Site by fully exploring the site's history!
