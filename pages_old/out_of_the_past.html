Out of the Past is an achievement that requires the player to give Pastkeeper Sharrigan her mentor's Pastkeeper amulet.

Pastkeeper Sharrigan can be found inside the Dream of Iaia (near the hibernation pod). Talking to Sharrigan in the Anachronia base camp will not work.
