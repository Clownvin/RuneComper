Lovingly Crafted is an achievement that requires the player to obtain and read all five horror journals. The blasphemous journal has to be obtained last.

Horror journals Sources
Decaying journal Guaranteed drop from skeletal horror.
Festering journal Uncommon drop from unspeakable horrors.
Rotten journal Complete the hooded pirate's miniquest.
Pestilent journal Found in the north-west of Mos Le'Harmless in a jungle plant.
Blasphemous journal Found in mound of soil north-east of the wheat field in Mos Le'Harmless after finding and reading the other four journals.

    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The achievement's name is a reference to the author H. P. Lovecraft, who pioneered the Lovecraftian horror genre.
