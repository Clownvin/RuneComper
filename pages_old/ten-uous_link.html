Ten-uous Link is an achievement that requires the player to reach a score of 10 using the strange switches in the Wilderness. The switches are located near the entrance to Daemonheim from the Wilderness east of the Chaos Temple. It is recommended to bring food and armour due to the Bound skeletons and Fetid zombies roaming the area. They can be partially avoided by taking a circular path around the east instead of taking a straight path between the levers.
