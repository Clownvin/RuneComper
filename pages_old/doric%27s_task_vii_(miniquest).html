Nostalgic Warhammers[edit | edit source]

Doric will tell you that Santiri wants you to smith a first generation warhammer. Head to the most north-western building in western Keldagrim. Talking to him before making the warhammer is optional. Talk to Santiri, he will tell you to talk to Supreme Commander Bisi in the building south of him up the stairs. Bisi will give you a black guard warhammer, and tell you to hit it with a hammer. Use the black warhammer on an anvil (one is located south of the building the Supreme Commander is in) to get the black guard warhammer (cracked).

Throughout this task, be sure to speak with Santiri and show him the warhammer at each stage (cracked, crushed, and rusty). If you skip showing the warhammer to him, you will need to destroy the item and start the process over. This will update the task list at each step.

Head back to Santiri, but he will say that it's an 18th generation warhammer at best, and suggests heading to the library. Go to the librarian, in the most north-eastern building in western Keldagrim, and he will suggest talking to someone used to working with heavy stone. Bring the warhammer to the Stonemason in the southernmost building in western Keldagrim, and he will suggest using his stone crusher to crush it. Use the warhammer on the stone crusher in the north-west corner of the building (you must use the item on the stone crusher, as the stone crusher has no left-click options), and you will now have a black guard warhammer (crushed).

Head back to Santiri, and he will still complain, saying it looks like a 6th generation warhammer. He suggests that it needs to be rustier, so use it on a water source. The closest usable ones are in eastern Keldagrim - use it on a well there to obtain the black guard warhammer (rusty).

Finally, head back to Santiri and he will be satisfied, and give you your reward.

If you talk with the Supreme Commander before speaking with Santiri, part of the story will be skipped.
