War. What Is It Good For? is an achievement that requires the player to complete the three other achievements associated with War's Retreat. This requires at least 2,000 boss kills, 98,500 Marks of War and 1,000 each of oak logs, willow logs, maple logs, yew logs, magic logs, and elder logs, valued at 11,524,000 coins at Grand Exchange.

Achievement Marks of War
My Last Resort ( 0) – Unlock the mentioned amenities in War's Retreat. 0
Relight My Fire ( 30) – Unlock the mentioned bonfire upgrades from War's store. 6,000
Aura Aura Aura! ( 30) – Unlock the mentioned auras from War's store. 92,500

    • Some of the other achievements from War's Retreat have been shifted around and had various adjustments to their Runescore, but the total amount of Runescore obtained from all of these achievement remains the same. This means this achievement was reduced from 30 RuneScore to 0.
  • update 27 January 2020 (Update):
    • Added to game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This achievement's name is a reference to the lyrics of War, a song by The Temptations.
