Civil War II (miniquest) is the third miniquest in the Legacy of Zamorak quest series concerning the Zamorakian Civil War. It serves as a narrative framework for the Wilderness Flash Events and is a requirement for the tier 4 upgrade of the Infernal Puzzle Box.

Contacting Anne Dimitri[edit | edit source]

Anne Dimitri has located another recording on one of her Praetorian devices, but it had been stolen by the Wandering Ramokee and Nikkel the imp. She asks the player to recover it so she can obtain some crucial information, and suggests theft or murder, though the World Guardian suggests trying the diplomatic approach first.

The Wandering Ramokee and Nikkel can be found to the north-east Edgeville, by the Deathmatch entrance on the south side of the Wilderness wall.

Upon talking to either of the duo, they will confess to having obtained the device, but will not give it up until the player makes an informed decision about which faction of the war is 'better', the demonic faction or the Daemonheim faction. Players must complete at least one Wilderness Flash Event to make an "informed decision", at which point they can make a choice; this choice will not affect the rest of the miniquest. They will give the device back, and players will simply bring it back to Anne, who is irritated they damaged the device, causing it to only play in black and white.

The recording[edit | edit source]

The flashback is set in the Wilderness somewhere, where three Zamorakian cultists are sitting at a campfire waiting for a fourth cultist to return. The first cultist, Lux begins telling a campfire story about Vengeance, and the terrible anger and madness she went through from having her brother murdered by adventurers, before roaming the Wilderness for more people to kill. The second cultist, Solmestix, a recent recruit from Taverley, is scared by the tale, but the third cultist, Jaxyys, interrupts with her own version of the tale, which focused on how much trauma Vengeance went through and her eventual escape and recovery. As the tales are told and mixed, Solmestix keeps seeing glimpses of Vengeance in the corner of his eye, until she appears and kills both Lux and Jaxyys. She approaches Solmastix to do the same, who screams for mercy and begs to see his sister one last time. Feeling a sense of familiarity with familial bond, Vengeance tells him to leave the Wilderness and never return, and to go see his sister. He runs out crying towards the Wilderness wall.

Anne is pleased to hear about the story of Vengeance being true (as well as a particular painting of Zamorak and a chaise lounge), and considers the information vital. She thanks the player for their efforts.
