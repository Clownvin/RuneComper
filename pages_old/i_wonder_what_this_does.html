I Wonder What This Does is an achievement that requires the player to teleport to the Wilderness using the lever in Ardougne which is located in a building along the eastern side of the wall that separates East Ardougne from West Ardougne (marked with a transportation symbol on the map). It will take the player to the Deserted Keep in the deep Wilderness. Don't bring anything that one is not willing to lose. Players can quickly teleport back to Ardougne by pulling the lever right next to where the first lever takes them. However, there is no guarantee players can teleport back without being attacked or killed.
