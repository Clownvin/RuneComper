Queen of the Icyene is a mystery in which the lost pages of Queen Efaritay's journal must be located. Solving the mystery completes the achievement of the same name.

Overview[edit | edit source]

Alignment Saradominist
Dig site Everlight Dig Site
Requirements
  • 42 Archaeology [B]
Required items These items can be obtained before and/or during the mystery.
  • Queen's journal page 1
  • Queen's journal page 2
  • Queen's journal page 3
  • Queen's journal page 4
Rewards 2,100 experience

Steps[edit | edit source]

  • Find one of the four Queen’s journal pages from the prodromoi remains or the monoceros remains excavation hotspots at the Everlight dig site.
  • Talk to Vanescula Drakan at the base camp of the Everlight dig site if you wish.
  • Collect the other three Queen's journal pages from the prodromoi remains or the monoceros remains excavation hotspots at the Everlight dig site.
  • Once all four pages are found, speak with Vanescula again. If asked if you want to speak about Fallen Angels or [more...], choose more, and the mystery will automatically complete.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The player Airut Bot was the first player who completed this mystery on 2 April 2020 which was shown in-game with the announcement: News: Airut Bot is the first to solve the 'Queen of the Icyene' mystery!
  • "Queen of the Icyene" is a reference to Celtic heroine Boudica (1st c. AD), who was Queen of the Iceni tribe.
