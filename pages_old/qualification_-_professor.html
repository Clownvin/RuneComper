Certificate of Qualification (Professor)

Qualification - Professor is an achievement that requires the player to complete specific achievements and talking to Acting Guildmaster Reiniger at the Archaeology Guild.

Completing this achievement rewards the player with an increased research team size of four, access to research associates, the Professor tab rewards in the Archaeology Reward Shop, new special research, and the title, [Name] the Archaeology Professor.

After completing the achievement four material manuals, four hi-spec monocles, four tarpaulin sheets, and four cups of archaeologist's tea may be claimed from Acting Guildmaster Reiniger by talking to her.
