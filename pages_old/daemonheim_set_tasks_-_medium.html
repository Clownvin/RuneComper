Daemonheim Set Tasks - Medium
Release date 18 April 2017 (Update)
Members Yes
Description Given by Marmaros, the Rewards Trader, in the camp for completing all Medium Tasks in Daemonheim.
RuneScore 10
Category Area Tasks
Rewards
  • Daemonheim aura 2
  • Magic lamp worth 8,000 XP in a skill of your choice.
  • A one off payment of 2,000 Dungeoneering tokens.
  • The aura gains the following extra benefits:
    • Ability to bind one potion in a new slot.
    • A supply of law and cosmic runes available from the Smuggler for free in every dungeon.
    • Secondary ring role effect is boosted to 50%
    • Access to an extended portion of the resource dungeon on the Daemonheim Peninsula.
  • Whilst wearing the aura:
    • The aura will act like Ava's Accumulator and the bonecrusher, whilst inside Daemonheim.
Requirements
  • See requirements

Daemonheim Set Tasks - Medium is an achievement that is rewarded upon finishing the Medium Daemonheim achievements.
